PDhS_12.1^4–12.2	 yathā pramāṇataḥ kāraṇa anupalabdheḥ nityaḥ bhāvaḥ iti uktam tathā samavāyaḥ api iti na hi asya kiñcid kāraṇam pramāṇataḥ upalabhyate iti kayā punar vṛttyā dravya ādiṣu samavāyaḥ vartate na samyogaḥ sambhavati tasya guṇa tvena dravya āśrita tvāt na api samavāyaḥ tasya ekatvāt na ca anyā vṛttiḥ asti iti na tādātmyāt yathā dravya guṇa karmaṇām sat ātmakasya bhāvasya na anyaḥ sattā yogaḥ asti evam avibhāginaḥ vṛtti ātmakasya samavāyasya na anyā vṛttiḥ asti tasmāt sva ātma vṛttiḥ atas eva atīndriyaḥ sattā ādīnām iva pratyakṣeṣu vṛtti abhāvāt sva ātma gata saṃvedana abhāvāt ca tasmāt ihabuddhi anumeyaḥ samavāyaḥ iti iti praśasta pāda bhāṣye samavāya padārthaḥ samāptaḥ yoga ācāra vibhūtyā yaḥ toṣayitvā maheśvaram cakre vaiśeṣikam śāstram tasmai kaṇabhuje namaḥ iti praśasta pāda viracitam dravya ādi ṣaṭpadārtha bhāṣyam samāptam
