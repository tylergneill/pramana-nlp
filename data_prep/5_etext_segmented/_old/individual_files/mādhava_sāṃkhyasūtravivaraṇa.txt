SSV_10	 pañca abhibuddhayaḥ atha kāḥ tāḥ abhibuddhayaḥ iti ucyante pañca abhibuddhayaḥ buddhiḥ abhimānaḥ icchā kartavyam tad kriyā ca iti abhimukhī buddhiḥ abhibuddhiḥ idam karaṇīyam eti etat adhyavasāyaḥ buddhiḥ kriyā ātma parāmarśa svarūpa pratyayaḥ abhimukhaḥ mānaḥ aham karomi iti ahaṃkāraḥ abhimānaḥ buddhi kriyā icchā vāñchā kartavyam saṃkalpaḥ manasaḥ vṛttiḥ kriyā etat vastu svarūpam iti kartavyā ālocanam yat buddhīndriyāṇām sā kriyā ittham etat kartavyam iti yat pravṛttiḥ karmendriyāṇām sā kriyā iti evam eta āha pañca abhibuddhayaḥ vyākhyātāḥ 
SSV_11	 pañca karma yonayaḥ atha kāḥ pañcakarma yonayaḥ ucyante dhṛtiḥ śraddhā sukhā avividiṣā vividiṣā ca iti pañca karma yonayaḥ vāci karmaṇi kāye ca pratītiḥ yābhiḥ ajyate tad niṣṭhaḥ tad pratiṣṭhaḥ ca dhṛteḥ etat hi lakṣaṇam anasūyā brahmacaryam yajanam yājanam tapaḥ pratigrahaḥ atra homaḥ ca śraddhāyāḥ lakṣaṇam smṛtam sukha arthī yaḥ tu seveta vidyā karma tapāṃsi ca prāyaścitta paraḥ nityam sukheyam parikīrtitā viṣa dhūma mūrchita vat avividiṣā vividiṣā dhyāyinām prajñā yoniḥ eka tvam ca pṛthaktvam ca nityam ca idam acetanam sūkṣmam satkāryam akṣobhyam jñeyā vividiṣā hi sā kārya kāraṇa kṣamakarā vividiṣā prākṛtikā vṛttiḥ dhṛtiḥ śraddhā sukhā avividiṣā catasraḥ bandhāya vividiṣā ekā mokṣāya iti pañca karma yonayaḥ vyākhyātāḥ 
SSV_12	 pañca vāyavaḥ atra āha ke te pañca vāyavaḥ ucyante prāṇaḥ apānaḥ samānaḥ ca udānaḥ vyānaḥ eva ca iti ete vāyavaḥ pañca śarīreṣu śarīriṇām tatra prāṇaḥ nāma vāyuḥ yaḥ ayam mukha nāsikā adhiṣṭhāna prāṇanāt krameṇa ānanāt prāṇaḥ iti abhidhīyate apānaḥ nāma vāyuḥ nābheḥ adhiṣṭhātā apanayanāt adhas gamanāt cāpānaḥ iti abhidhīyate samānaḥ nāma vāyuḥ hṛd adhiṣṭhātā samanāt sama gamanāt ca samānaḥ iti abhidhīyate udānaḥ nāma vāyuḥ kaṇṭha adhiṣṭhātā ūrdhvam gamanāt ca udānaḥ iti abhidhīyate vyānaḥ nāma vāyuḥ sandhī adhiṣṭhātā prāṇa ādi jṛmbhaṇāt ca vyānaḥ iti abhidhīyate iti pañca vāyavaḥ vyākhyātāḥ 
SSV_13	 pañca karma ātmānaḥ āha ke te pañcakarma ātmānaḥ ucyante vaikārikaḥ taijasaḥ bhūtādiḥ ca sa anumānaḥ niranumānaḥ ca iti tatra vaikārikaḥ śubha karma kartā taijasaḥ aśubha karma kartā bhūtādiḥ mūḍha karma kartā sānumānaḥ śubha mūḍha karma kartā niranumānaḥ aśubha mūḍha karma kartā iti ete pañcakarma kartāraḥ vyākhyātāḥ 
SSV_14	 pañca parva avidyā āha kā sā pañca parvā avidyā iti ucyate tamaḥ mohaḥ mahāmohaḥ tāmisraḥ andhatāmisraḥ ca iti tatra tamaḥ mohau= ubhau= etau= aṣṭa ātmākau mahāmohaḥ daśa ātmakaḥ tāmisra andhatāmisrau ca aṣṭādaśa ātmākau tatra tamaḥ nāma aṣṭāsu prakṛtiṣu avyakta buddhi ahaṃkāra pañca tanmātra sañjñitāsu anātmasva ātma jñāna abhimānaḥ tamaḥ iti abhidhīyate mohaḥ nāma aṇima ādi aṣṭa aguṇa aiśvarya prāptaye yobhimānaḥ utpadyate sa mohaḥ iti abhidhīyate mahāmohaḥ nāma dṛṣṭa ānuśravikeṣu viṣayeṣu daśasu nivṛtteṣu muktaḥ aham iti manyate sa mahāmohaḥ iti abhidhīyate tāmisraḥ nāma aṣṭaguṇa aiśvarye aṇima ādye daśavidhe ca viṣaye pratihatasya duḥkham utpadyate asau tāmisraḥ iti abhidhīyate andhatāmisraḥ nāma aṇima ādi aṣṭaguṇa aiśvarye daśavidhe ca viṣaye siddhe maraṇa kāle ca yaḥ viṣādaḥ pratyapadyate saḥ andhatāmisraḥ iti abhidhīyate evam eṣā pañca parvā avidyā dvi ṣaṣṭi bhedāḥ vyākhyātāḥ 
SSV_15	 aṣṭaviṃśatidhā śaktiḥ atra āha kāṣṭāviṃśatidhā śaktiḥ atra ucyate ekādaśa indriya vadhāḥ saptadaśa buddhi vadhāḥ eṣā aṣṭāviṃśatidhā śaktiḥ tatra indriya vadhāḥ tāvat badhiraḥ tvakbadhiraḥ andhaḥ jaḍa jihvaḥ ajighraḥ mūkaḥ kuṇiḥ paṅguḥ napuṃsakaḥ ūrdhva vāyu yuktaḥ amanāḥ tat uktam bādhiryam kuṣṭhita andhaḥ tvam jaḍatā ajighratā tathā mūka tā kauṇya paṅgu tvam klaibya udāvarta matta tāḥ
