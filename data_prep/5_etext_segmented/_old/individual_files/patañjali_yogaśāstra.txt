YŚ_2.18.3	 tau etau bhoga apavargau buddhi kṛtau buddhau eva vartamānau katham puruṣe vyapadiśyete iti yathā vijayaḥ parājayaḥ vā yoddhṛṣu vartamānaḥ svāmini vyapadiśyate sa hi tad phalasya bhoktā iti evam bandha mokṣau buddhau eva vartamānau puruṣe vyapadiśyete sa hi tad phalasya bhoktā iti buddheḥ eva puruṣa artha aparisamāptiḥ bandhaḥ tad artha avasāyaḥ mokṣaḥ iti etena grahaṇa dhāraṇa ūha apoha tattva jñāna abhiniveśāḥ buddhau vartamānāḥ puruṣe adhyāropita sadbhāvāḥ sa hi tad phalasya bhoktā iti

YŚ_4.22.2–4.23.1	 na pātālam na ca vivaram girīṇām na eva andhakāram kukṣayaḥ na udadhīnām guhā yasyām nihitam brahma śāśvatam buddhi vṛttim aviśiṣṭām kavayaḥ vedayante iti atas ca etat abhyupagamyate draṣṭṛ dṛśya uparaktam cittam sarva artham manaḥ hi mantavyena arthena uparaktam tat svayam ca viṣaya tvāt viṣayiṇā puruṣeṇa ātmīyayā vṛttyā abhisaṃbaddham tat etat cittam eva draṣṭṛ dṛśya uparaktam viṣaya viṣayi nirbhāsam cetana acetana svarūpa āpannam viṣaya ātmakam api aviṣaya ātmakam iva acetanam cetanam iva sphaṭika maṇi kalpam sarva artham iti ucyate 
YŚ_4.23.2	 tat anena citta sārūpyeṇa bhrāntāḥ kecid tat eva cetanam iti āhuḥ apare cittamātram eva idam sarvam na asti khalu ayam gavādiḥ ghaṭa ādiḥ ca sa kāraṇaḥ lokaḥ iti anukampanīyāḥ te kasmāt asti hi teṣām bhrānti bījam sarva rūpa ākāra nirbhāsam cittam iti samādhi prajñāyām prajñeyaḥ arthaḥ pratibimbībhūtaḥ tasya ālambanībhūta tvāt anyaḥ sa ced arthaḥ cittamātram syāt katham prajñayā eva prajñā rūpam avadhāryeta tasmāt pratibimbībhūtaḥ arthaḥ prajñāyām yena avadhāryate sa puruṣaḥ iti evam grahītṛ grahaṇa grāhya svarūpa citta bhedāt trayam api etat jātitaḥ pravibhajante te samyak darśinaḥ taiḥ adhigataḥ puruṣaḥ 
YŚ_4.23.3–4.25.0	 kutas ca tat asaṃkhyeya vāsanābhiḥ citram api para artham saṃhatyakāri tvāt tat etat cittam asaṃkhyeyābhiḥ vāsanābhiḥ eva citrīkṛtam api para artham parasya bhoga apavarga artham na sva artham saṃhatyakāri tvāt gṛha vat saṃhatya kāriṇā cittena na sva arthena bhavitavyam na sukha cittam sukha artham na jñānam jñāna artham ubhayam api etat para artham yaḥ ca bhogena apavargeṇa ca arthena arthavān puruṣaḥ saḥ eva paraḥ na paraḥ sāmānya mātram yat tu kiṃcid param sāmānya mātram svarūpeṇa udāharet vaināśikaḥ tat sarvam saṃhatyakāri tvāt para artham eva syāt yaḥ tu asau paraḥ viśeṣaḥ sa na saṃhatya kārī puruṣaḥ iti viśeṣa darśinaḥ ātma bhāva bhāvanā nivṛttiḥ 
YŚ_4.25.1	 yathā prāvṛṣi tṛṇa aṅkurasya udbhedena tat bīja sattā anumīyate tathā mokṣa mārga śravaṇena yasya roma harṣa aśru pātau dṛśyete tatra api asti viśeṣa darśana bījam apavarga bhāgīyam karma abhinirvartitam iti anumīyate tasya ātma bhāva bhāvanā svābhāvikī pravartate yasya abhāvāt idam uktam svabhāvam muktvā doṣa ādi eṣām pūrva pakṣe ruciḥ bhavati aruciḥ ca nirṇaye bhavati tatra ātma bhāva bhāvanā kaḥ aham āsam katham aham āsam kiṃsvid idam kathaṃsvid idam ke bhaviṣyāmaḥ katham vā bhaviṣyāmaḥ iti sā tu viśeṣa darśinaḥ nivartate kutas cittasya eva eṣa vicitraḥ pariṇāmaḥ puruṣaḥ tu asatyām avidyāyām śuddhaḥ citta dharmaiḥ aparāmṛṣṭaḥ iti tatas asya ātma bhāva bhāvanā kuśalasya nivartate iti 
YŚ_4.26.0–4.27.1	 tadā viveka nimnam kaivalya prāgbhāram cittam tadānīm yat asya cittam viṣaya prāgbhāram ajñāna nimnam āsīt tat asya anyathā bhavati kaivalya prāgbhāram viveka ja jñāna nimnam iti tad chidreṣu pratyaya antarāṇi saṃskārebhyaḥ pratyaya viveka nimnasya sattva puruṣa anya tā khyāti mātra pravāhiṇaḥ cittasya tad chidreṣu pratyaya antarāṇi asmi iti vā mama iti vā jānāmi iti vā na jānāmi iti vā kutas kṣīyamāṇa bījebhyaḥ pūrva saṃskārebhyaḥ iti 
YŚ_4.28.0–4.29.0	 hānam eṣām kleśa vat uktam yathā kleśāḥ dagdha bīja bhāvāḥ na praroha samarthāḥ bhavanti tathā jñāna agninā dagdha bīja bhāvaḥ pūrva saṃskāraḥ na pratyaya prasūḥ bhavati jñāna saṃskārāḥ tu citta adhikāra samāptim anuśerate iti na cintyante prasaṃkhyāne api akusīdasya sarvathā viveka khyāteḥ dharma meghaḥ samādhiḥ 
YŚ_4.29.1–4.30.1	 yadā ayam brāhmaṇaḥ prasaṃkhyāne api akusīdaḥ tatas api na kiṃcid prārthayate tatra api viraktasya sarvathā viveka khyātiḥ eva bhavati iti saṃskāra bīja kṣayān na asya pratyaya antarāṇi utpadyante tadā asya dharma meghaḥ nāma samādhiḥ bhavati tatas kleśa karma nivṛttiḥ tad lābhāt avidyā ādayaḥ kleśāḥ sa mūla kāṣam kaṣitāḥ bhavanti kuśala akuśalāḥ ca karma āśayāḥ sa mūla ghātam hatāḥ bhavanti kleśa karma nivṛttau jīvan eva vidvān vimuktaḥ bhavati kasmāt yasmāt viparyayaḥ bhavasya kāraṇam na hi kṣīṇa viparyayaḥ kaścid kenacid kvacid jātaḥ dṛśyate iti 
YŚ_4.31.0–4.31.1	 tadā sarva āvaraṇa mala apetasya jñānasya ānantyāt jñeyam alpam sarvaiḥ kleśa karma āvaraṇaiḥ vimuktasya jñānasya ānantyam bhavati āvarakeṇa tamasā abhibhūtam āvṛtam anantam jñāna sattvam kvacid eva rajasā pravartitam udghāṭitam grahaṇa samartham bhavati tatra yadā sarvaiḥ āvaraṇa malaiḥ apagatam bhavati tadā bhavati asya ānantyam jñānasya ānantyāt jñeyam alpam saṃpadyate yathā ākāśe khadyotaḥ yatra idam uktam 
YŚ_4.31.2–4.33.1	 andhaḥ maṇim avidhyat tam anaṅguliḥ āvayat agrīvaḥ tam pratyamuñcat tam ajihvaḥ abhyapūjayat iti tatas kṛtārthānām pariṇāma krama samāptiḥ guṇānām tasya dharma meghasya udayāt kṛtārthānām guṇānām pariṇāma kramaḥ parisamāpyate na hi kṛta bhoga apavargāḥ parisamāpta kramāḥ kṣaṇam api avasthātum utsahante atha kaḥ ayam kramaḥ nāma iti kṣaṇa pratiyogī pariṇāma apara anta nirgrāhyaḥ kramaḥ kṣaṇa anantari ātmā pariṇāmasya aparāntena avasānena gṛhyate kramaḥ na hi ananubhūta krama kṣaṇā purāṇa tā vastrasya ante bhavati nityeṣu ca kramaḥ dṛṣṭaḥ 
YŚ_4.33.2	 dvayī ca iyam nityatā kūṭastha nityatā pariṇāmi nityatā ca tatra kūṭastha nityatā puruṣasya pariṇāmi nityatā guṇānām yasmin pariṇamyamāne tattvam na vihanyate tat nityam ubhayasya ca tattva anabhighātāt nitya tvam tatra guṇa dharmeṣu buddhi ādiṣu pariṇāma apara anta nirgrāhyaḥ kramaḥ labdha paryavasānaḥ nityeṣu dharmiṣu guṇeṣu alabdha paryavasānaḥ kūṭastha nityeṣu sva rūpa mātra pratiṣṭheṣu mukta puruṣeṣu svarūpa astitā krameṇa eva anubhūyata iti tatra api alabdha paryavasānaḥ śabda pṛṣṭhena asti kriyām upādāya kalpitaḥ iti 
YŚ_4.33.3–4.33.4	 atha asya saṃsārasya sthityā gatyā ca guṇeṣu vartamānasya asti krama samāptiḥ na vā iti avacanīyam etat katham asti praśnaḥ ekānta vacanīyaḥ sarvaḥ jātaḥ mariṣyati iti om bhoḥ iti atha sarvaḥ mṛtvā janiṣyate iti vibhajya vacanīyam etat pratyudita khyātiḥ kṣīṇa tṛṣṇaḥ kuśalaḥ na janiṣyate itaraḥ tu janiṣyate tathā manuṣya jātiḥ śreyasī na vā śreyasi iti evam paripṛṣṭe vibhajya vacanīyaḥ praśnaḥ paśūn adhikṛtya śreyasī devān ṛṣīn ca adhikṛtya na iti ayam tu avacanīyaḥ praśnaḥ saṃsāraḥ ayam antavān atha anantaḥ iti kuśalasya asti saṃsāra krama samāptiḥ na itarasya iti anyatara avadhāraṇe doṣaḥ tasmāt vyākaraṇīyaḥ eva ayam praśnaḥ iti
