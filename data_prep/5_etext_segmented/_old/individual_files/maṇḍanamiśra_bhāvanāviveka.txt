BhV_7^1	 karmaṇaḥ bhāva bhedam yaḥ phala avadhi tayā vadet tasya adṛṣṭa phaleṣu iṣṭam anuṣṭhānam na yuktimat dhātuḥ hi karma sva rūpa mātreṇa upādatte ākhyāta upasaṃdhānāt tu bhāvanā ātmanā avagamyate tathā hi yāvatā karma kṣaṇa utpāda nicayena iṣṭam artham sādhayati tāvatī ākhyātam prayujyate atas tena tena phalena avacchidyamānaḥ sa sa karma kṣaṇa utpāda nicayaḥ tena tena ākhyātena abhidhīyate tat idam phalau= adhikam karmam bhāvyatayā avasīyate na abhāvyamānam phala bhāvanāya alam iti phala utpādikāḥ avasthā ākhyāta arthaḥ tathā yateta yathā yat kiñcid bhavati iti darśitam evam avasthā bhedena bhāvanā karmaṇoḥ bhedaḥ 
BhV_7^2	 uktam ca pūrva aparībhūtam bhāvam ākhyātena ācaṣṭe vrajati pacati iti upakrama prabhṛti apavarga paryantam iti tatra pratikṣaṇam apavargavataḥ karmaṇaḥ kau upakrama apavargau phala apekṣayā tu kalpyete iti yaḥ vadet tasya pratyakṣa phaleṣu karmasu kāmam ārambha virāmau kalpyete puras parastāt ca tad paryanta karma kṣaṇa pracaya parijñānāt adṛṣṭa phaleṣu tu anirūpita phala paryanta parimāṇa bhedeṣu katham pravartate na hi niyuktaḥ api aprasiddha viṣaya viśeṣaḥ pravartitum prabhavati pravṛttau vā puras phala adarśanāt akṛta śāstra artham ātmānam avabudhyamānaḥ na virontum arhati śāstra arthasya phala utpatteḥ phala utpattyā ca kṛta śāstra artha tvāt itaretara āśraya prasaṅgāt iti alam prasaṅgena

BhV_9	 api ca na bhāve na api dhātu arthe kāṣṭhaiḥ iti samāgamaḥ akriyā tvāt anya rodhāt śābda tvāt karaṇasya ca na tāvat dhātu artha saṃbandhi tā kāṣṭhānām karaṇānām kriyā apekṣiṇām atada ātmanā karaṇa antareṣṇa saṃgamā yogāt na api bhāvanā saṃbandhi tā tasyāḥ karaṇa antara uparuddha tvāt na api aṃśa antara tayā saṃbandhaḥ śābda tvāt karaṇa tvasya tat evam yatra karaṇa bhāvaḥ kriyāyām tasyāḥ avaruddha tvāt dhātu arthe ca akriyā ātmani akaraṇa tvāt kāṣṭhaiḥ pacati iti saṃbandha abhāvaḥ artha kṣipta bhāvanā dvārakaḥ saṃbandhaḥ iti ced na avyavadhānena saṃbandha bodhanāt na hi mṛdā ghaṭam kāṣṭhaiḥ pacati iti samaḥ saṃbandha avagamaḥ ekatra naiḥ ākāṅkṣyāt anyatra anya apekṣaṇāt
