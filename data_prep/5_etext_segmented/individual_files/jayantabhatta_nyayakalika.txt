[7.2.3_7.2.3.2.1]	dṛṣṭānta vacanam udāharaṇam dṛṣṭāntaḥ dvividhaḥ sādharmyeṇa vaidharmyeṇa ca yatra prayojya prayojaka bhāvena sādhya sādhana dharmayoḥ astitvam khyāpyate sa sādharmya dṛṣṭāntaḥ tasya vyāpya vyāpaka bhāva garbham vacanam udāharaṇam yathā yat yat kṛtakam tat tat anityam dṛṣṭam yathā ghaṭaḥ iti 
[7.2.3.2.2_7.2.3.3.1]	yatra sādhya abhāva prayuktaḥ hetu abhāvaḥ khyāpyate sa vaidharmya dṛṣṭāntaḥ tasya tathāvidham eva vacanam udāharaṇam yatra anitya tvam na asti tatra kṛtaka tvam api na asti yathā ākāśaḥ iti arthāt ca dṛṣṭānta ābhāsāḥ pratikṣiptāḥ veditavyāḥ sādharmya dṛṣṭānta ābhāse trayaḥ artha doṣāḥ sādhya vikalaḥ sādhana vikalaḥ ubhaya vikalaḥ dvau vacana doṣau ananvayaḥ viparīta anvayaḥ iti
