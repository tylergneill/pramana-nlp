NPrS_2.2.1–2.2.4	 kaḥ punar sa pakṣaḥ kaḥ vā vipakṣaḥ iti sādhya dharma sāmānyena samānaḥ arthaḥ sa pakṣaḥ tat yathā anitye śabde sādhye ghaṭa ādiḥ anityaḥ sa pakṣaḥ vipakṣaḥ yatra sādhyam na asti yat nityaḥ tat akṛtakam dṛṣṭam yathā ākāśam iti tatra kṛtaka tvam prayatna anantarīyaka tvam vā sa pakṣaḥ eva asti vipakṣe na asti eva iti anitya ādau hetuḥ 
NPrS_2.3–2.4	 dṛṣṭāntaḥ dvividhaḥ sādharmyeṇa vaidharmyeṇa ca tatra sādharmyeṇa tāvat yatra hetoḥ sa pakṣaḥ eva astitvam khyāpyate tat yathā yat kṛtakam tat anityam dṛṣṭam yathā ghaṭa ādiḥ iti vaidharmyeṇa api yatra sādhya abhāve hetoḥ abhāva kathyate tat yathā yat nityam tat akṛtakam dṛṣṭam yathā ākāśām iti nitya śabdena atra anitya tvasya abhāvaḥ ucyate akṛtaka śabdena api kṛtaka tvasya abhāvaḥ yathā bhāva abhāvaḥ abhāvaḥ iti uktāḥ pakṣa ādayaḥ eṣām vacanāni para pratyāyana kāle sādhanam tat yathā anityaḥ śabdaḥ iti pakṣa vacanam yat kṛtakam tat anityam dṛṣṭam yathā ghaṭa ādiḥ iti sa pakṣa anugama vacanam yat nityam tat akṛtakam dṛṣṭam yathā ākāśam iti vyatireka vaccanam etāni eva trayaḥ avayavāḥ iti ucyante 
NPrS_3.1–3.1.1	 sādhayitum iṣṭaḥ api pratyakṣa ādi viruddhaḥ pakṣa ābhāsaḥ tat yathā pratyakṣa viruddhaḥ anumāna viruddhaḥ āgama viruddhaḥ loka viruddhaḥ sva vat cana viruddhaḥ aprasiddha viśeṣaṇaḥ aprasiddha viśeṣyaḥ aprasiddha ubhayaḥ prasiddha saṃbandhaḥ ca iti tatra pratyakṣa viruddhaḥ yathā aśrāvaṇaḥ śabdaḥ iti anumāna viruddhaḥ yathā nityaḥ ghaṭaḥ iti āgama viruddhaḥ yathā vaiśeṣikasya nityaḥ śabdaḥ iti sādhyataḥ loka viruddhaḥ yathā śuci nara śiraḥ kapālam prāṇi aṅga tvāt śaṅkha śukti vat iti sva vacana viruddhaḥ yathā mātā me vandhyā iti aprasiddha viśeṣaṇaḥ yathā bauddhasya sāṃkhyam prati vināśī śabdaḥ iti aprasiddha viśeṣyaḥ yathā sāṃkhyasya bauddham prati cetanaḥ ātmā iti aprasiddha ubhayaḥ yathā vaiśeṣikasya bauddham prati sukha ādi samavāyi kāraṇam ātmā iti prasiddha saṃbandhaḥ yathā śrāvaṇaḥ śabdaḥ iti eṣām vacanāni dharma svarūpa nirākaraṇa mukhena pratipādana asaṃbhavataḥ sādhana vaiphalyataḥ ca iti pratijñā doṣāḥ uktāḥ pakṣa ābhāsāḥ 
NPrS_3.2–3.2.1.1	 asiddha anaikāntika viruddhāḥ hetvābhāsāḥ tatra asiddhaḥ catuḥ prakāraḥ tat yathā ubhaya asiddhaḥ anyatara asiddhaḥ saṃdigdha asiddhaḥ āśraya asiddhaḥ ca iti tatra śabda anitya tve sādye cākṣuṣa tvāt iti ubhaya asiddhaḥ kṛtaka tvāt iiti śabda abhivyakti vādinam prati anyatara asiddhaḥ vā aṣpa ādi bhāvena saṃdihyamānaḥ bhūta saṃghātaḥ agni siddhau upadiśyamānaḥ saṃdigdha asiddhaḥ dravyam ākāśam guṇa āśraya tvāt iti ākāśa asattva vādinam prati āśraya asiddhaḥ 
NPrS_3.2.2–3.2.2.2	 anaikāntikaḥ ṣaṣ prakāraḥ sādhāraṇaḥ asādhāraṇaḥ sa pakṣa eka deśa vṛttiḥ vipakṣa vyāpī vipakṣa eka deśa vṛttiḥ sa pakṣa vyāpī ubhaya pakṣa eka deśa vṛttiḥ viruddha avyabhicārī ca iti tatra sādhāraṇaḥ śabdaḥ pprameya tvāt nityaḥ iti tat dhi nitya anitya pakṣayoḥ sādhāraṇa tvāt anaikāntikam kim ghaṭa vat prameya tvāt anityaḥ śabdaḥ āhosvit ākāśa vat prameya tvāt nityaḥ iti asādhāraṇaḥ śrāvaṇa tvāt nityaḥ iti tat dhi nityān iti apakṣabhyām vyāvṛtta tvāt nitya anitya vinirmuktasya ca anyasya asaṃbhavāt saṃśaya hetuḥ kiṃbhūtasya asya śrāvaṇa tvam iti 
NPrS_3.2.2.3	 sa pakṣa eka deśa vṛttiḥ vipakṣa vyāpī yathā aprayatna anantarīyakaḥ śabdaḥ anitya tvāt aprayatna ānantarīyakaḥ pakṣaḥ asya vidyut ākāśa ādiḥ sa pakṣaḥ tatra eka deśe vidyut ādau vidyate anitya tvam na ākāśa ādau aprayatna ānantarīyakaḥ pakṣaḥ asya ghaṭa ādiḥ vipakṣaḥ tatra sarvatra ghaṭa ādau vidyate anitya tvam tasmāt etat api vidyut ghaṭa sādharmyeṇa anaikāntikam kim ghaṭa vat anitya tvāt prayatna anantarīyakaḥ śabdaḥ āhosvit vidyut ādi vat anitya tvāt aprayatna anantarīyakaḥ iti 
NPrS_3.2.2.4	 vipakṣa eka deśa vṛttiḥ sa pakṣa vyāpī yathā prayatna ānantarīyakaḥ śabdaḥ anitya tvāt prayatna ānantarīyakaḥ pakṣaḥ asya ghaṭa ādiḥ sa pakṣaḥ tatra sarvatra ghaṭa ādau vidyate anitya tvam prayatna ānantarīyakaḥ pakṣaḥ asya vidyut ākāśa ādiḥ vipakṣaḥ tatra eka deśe vidyut ādau vidyate anitya tvam na ākāśa ādau tasmāt etat api vidyut ghaṭa sādharmyeṇa pūrvavat anaikāntikam 
NPrS_3.2.2.5	 ubhaya pakṣa eka deśa vṛttiḥ yathā nityaḥ śabdaḥ amūrta tvāt nityaḥ pakṣaḥ asya ākāśa paramāṇu ādiḥ sa pakṣaḥ tatra eka deśaḥ ākāśa ādau vidyate amūrta tvam paramāṇu ādau nityaḥ pakṣaḥ asya ghaṭa sukha ādiḥ vipakṣaḥ tatrika deśe sukha ādau vidyate amūrta tvam na ghaṭa ādau tasmāt etat api sukha ākāśa sādharmyeṇa anaikāntikam 
NPrS_3.2.2.6–3.2.3	 viruddha avyabhhicārī yathā anityaḥ śabda kṛtaka tvāt ghaṭa vat nityaḥ śabdaḥ śrāvaṇa tvāt śabda tva vat iti ubhayoḥ saṃśaya hetu tvat dvau api etau ekaḥ anaikāntikaḥ samuditau eva viruddhaḥ catur prakāraḥ tat yathā dharma svarūpa viparīta sadhanaḥ dharma viśeṣa viparīta sādhanaḥ dharmi svarūpa vipparīta sadhanaḥ dharmi viśeṣa viparīta sādhanaḥ ca iti 
NPrS_3.2.3.1–3.2.3.2	 dharma svarūpa viparīta sadhanaḥ yathā nityaḥ śabdaḥ kṛtaka tvāt prayatna anantarīyaka tvāt vā iti ayam hetuḥ vipakṣaḥ eva bhāvāt viruddhaḥ dharma viśeṣa viparīta sādhanaḥ yathā para arthāḥ cakṣuḥ ādayaḥ saṃghāta tvāt śayana āsana ādi aṅga viśeṣa vat iti ayam hetuḥ yathā pārārthyam cakṣuḥ ādīnām sādhayati yathā saṃhata tvam api parasya ātmanaḥ sādhayati ubhayatra avyabhicārāt 
NPrS_3.2.3.3–3.2.3.4	 dharmi svarūpa viparīta sādhanaḥ yathā na dravyam na karma na guṇaḥ bhāvaḥ eka dravyavat tvāt guṇa karmasu ca bhāvāt sāmānya viśeṣa vat iti ayam hi hetuḥ yathā dravya ādi pratiṣedham bhāvasya sādhayati yathā bhāvasya abhāva tvam api sādhayati ubhayatra avyabhicārāt dharmi viśeṣa viparīta sādhanaḥ yathā ayam eva hetuḥ asmin eva pūrva pakṣe asya eva dharmiṇaḥ yaḥ viśeṣaḥ sat pratyaya kartṛ tvam nāma tad viparītam asat pratyaya kartṛ tvam api sādhayati ubhayatra avyabhicārāt 
NPrS_3.3–3.3.1.1	 dṛṣṭānta ābhāsaḥ dvividhaḥ sādharmyeṇa vaidharmyeṇa ca tatra sādharmyeṇa tāvat dṛṣṭānta ābhāsaḥ pañca prakāraḥ tat yathā sādhana dharma asiddhaḥ sādhya dharma asiddhaḥ ubhaya dharma asiddhaḥ ananvayaḥ viparītā anvayaḥ ca iti tatra sādhana dharma asiddhaḥ yathā nityaḥ śabdaḥ amūrta tvāt paramāṇu vat yat amūrtam tat nityam dṛṣṭam yathā paramāṇuḥ paramāṇau hi sādhyam nitya tvam asti sat nana dharmaḥ amūrta tvam na asti mūrta tvāt paramāṇūnām iti
