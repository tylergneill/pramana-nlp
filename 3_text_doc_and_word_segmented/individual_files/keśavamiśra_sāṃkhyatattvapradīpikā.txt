STPradīpikā_0.2	 sā ca caturviṃśatidhā prakṛteḥ prathamaḥ mahān iti vikāraḥ pariṇāmaḥ iti yāvat mahat tattvam eva buddhi tattvam iti abhidhīyate aham iti ahaṃkāra ākhyaḥ mahat tattvasya prathamaḥ vikāraḥ tad anantaram pañca tanmātrāḥ śabda sparśa rūpa rasa gandha ākhyā bhavati atha ākāśa ādi bhūta pañcakam atha ekādaśa akṣāṇi śrotra sparśana cakṣū rasana ghrāṇa vāc gudaliṅga kara caraṇa antaḥkaraṇāni ekādaśa indriyāṇi bhavanti tatra mahat tattvam ahaṃkāra tattvam śabda ādyāḥ pañca tanmātrā iti etat tattva saptakam prakṛti vikṛti bhāva āpannam pūrvam pūrvam prati kāryatvam uttarottaram prati kāraṇa tvam iti arthaḥ tathā hi mahat tattvam mūlaprakṛteḥ kāryam kāraṇam ca ahaṃkāra tattvasya ahaṃkāra tattvam tathā mahat tattvasya kāryam kāraṇam ca śabda tattva ākhyādi tanmātram prati tathā hi tat api pūrvam prati kāryam uttaram prati kāraṇam ca iti bodhyam ākāśa ādi bhūta pañcakam ekādaśa indriyāṇi iti ete punar ṣoḍaśa bhāvāḥ ca prakṛti janyāḥ eva na tu kasyacid prakṛtiḥ tattva antara upādāna rūpa tva abhāvāt sā iyam prakṛtiḥ acetanā pariṇāmi tvāt 
STPradīpikā_0.3^1	 nanu viṣayāṇām ghaṭa ādīnām cetana sambandhaḥ sākṣāt eva astu kim mahat ādi praṇālikayā iti ced tat kim citiḥ eva viṣaya bandhana svabhāvā tathā sati ca eti anya nitya tayā mokṣaḥ kadāpi na syāt atha caitanyam prakṛtau pratibimbitam prakṛti viṣayayoḥ tu sākṣāt sambandhaḥ syāt kim mahat ādinā iti ced tathā hi puruṣa vat nitya tayā punar anirmokṣa āpattiḥ eva atha citiḥ viṣaya sambaddha svabhāvāḥ mā astu viṣayāḥ eva citi sambaddha svabhāvāḥ santu tathā ca viṣaya tirobhāve mokṣaḥ syāt iti ced tarhi idam dṛṣṭam iti na syāt sarvathā citi sambandha svābhāvyena avyavadhānāt atha viṣayāḥ indriya dvārā citi sambaddha ātmānaḥ santu dṛṣṭa adṛṣṭa vyavahāra ādi sannikarṣa viprakarṣābhyām upapatsyata iti ced na vyāsaṅga anupapatteḥ indriya sambaddhe viṣaye jñāna utpatteḥ āvaśyaka tvāt 
STPradīpikā_0.3^2	 atha vyāsaṅga anupapattyā manaḥ kalpyatām kim ahaṃkāreṇa mā evam evam ca sati svasmin vyāghroham varāhoham iti na āropayet kiṃtu indriyam manaḥ dvārā citi sambaddham svakīyam nara tvam eva gṛhṇīyāt atha evam ahaṃkāroḥ tu svapna daśāyāmāropa artham buddhi tattve na mānam asti mā evam suṣupti avasthāyām manaḥ vat ahaṃkāra upi nivṛtta vyāpāraḥ eva tathā ca śvāsa prayojaka jīvana yoni yatnasya vyāpāraḥ syāt iti tad anurodhena buddhi tattvasya api āvaśyaka tvāt tasmāt buddhi tattvam parigamena sambaddhaḥ viṣayaḥ gaja turaga mahiṣa ādiḥ puruṣeṇa caitanyena sambadhyate tatas āsaṃsāra buddhi tattva nāśāt eva ca mokṣaḥ iti na anirmokṣaḥ 
STPradīpikā_0.4^1	 akāraṇam akāryam ca kūṭastha caitanya rūpaḥ puruṣaḥ atra ayam puruṣaḥ svayam jyotiḥ iti śrutyā tāvat sva prakāśam ātmanaḥ a bhidhīyate sva prakāśam ca vijñānam eva tena ātmanaḥ sva prakāśa tvam abhidadhatīyam śrutiḥ vijñāna ātmaka tām puruṣasya āvedayati na ca kṣaṇikasya jñānasya nitya puruṣa ātmaka tvam sambhavati iti jñānasya kṣaṇa bhaṅgura tva asiddheḥ vināśa vyavahārasya tava ghaṭa ākāśa divināśa vyavahāra vat aupādhika tvena api upapatteḥ puruṣa atirikta caitanya svīkartuḥ tava api mate jñānasya kṣaṇika tve māna abhāvaḥ anyathā kāla antare anubhūta smaraṇa anupapattiḥ 
STPradīpikā_0.4^2	 na ca saṃskāraḥ dvāram tad kalpanā apekṣayā jñānasya sthairya kalpanāyām lāghavāt nitya tvāt ca asau na kasyacid vikāraḥ na vā kāraṇam sva prakāśa tvāt yadi hi sva prakāśasya api asya prakṛti tvam āśrīyate tarhi etat vikṛti tvena abhimatasya api sva prakāśa tvam asya vā jaḍa tvam āpadyeta samānayoḥ eva prakṛti vikṛti bhāva upalambhāt bheda agrahāt ca niṣkriyepi puruṣe kartṛ tva abhimānaḥ evam sukha duḥkha ādi vyavahāra upi tathā hi pariśuddha caitanya vigrahaḥ prakṛti vikṛti bhāva anapeta taṭa sthaḥ puṣkara palāśa vat nirlepaḥ api antaḥkaraṇa ākāra pariṇata prakṛti tādātmyam aviveka lakṣaṇam avidyayā pratipadyate tatas ca itarā itara adhyāsāt antaḥkaraṇa āvṛttīnām duḥkha ādīnām puruṣe puruṣa dharmāṇām cit caitanya ādīnām antaḥkaraṇe adhyāsāt aham duḥkhī iti ādi vyavahāraḥ bhavati iti avidyā nām ātāt tvikī khyātiḥ sā ca tamaḥ guṇa udrekāt āvirbhavantī viviktayoḥ tādātmyam avabhāsayati 
STPradīpikā_0.4^3	 tatas ca uttarā iṣām asmitā rāga dveṣa abhiniveśānām kleśa śabda abhidheyānām hetuḥ avidyā iti gīyate tad siddham nirlepaḥ eva ayam iti asaṅgaḥ hi ayam iti śruteḥ upapadyate ca na ca kopyasya vyāpāraḥ iti ālocanam tāvat indriya vyāpāraḥ vikalpaḥ tu manasaḥ abhimāna ūhaṃkārasya kṛtī adhyavasāyaḥ buddheḥ atha kṛtī adhyavasāyaḥ kṛtimat tayā jñānam tat katham acetanāyāḥ buddheḥ iti buddhiḥ aṃśa trayavatī puruṣa uparāgaḥ viṣaya uparāgaḥ vyāpāra āveśaḥ ca iti bhavati hi mama idam kartavyam iti tatra mama iti aṃśaḥ cetanasya darpaṇa nirmalāyāḥ buddheḥ pratibimbaḥ upadhānam tat ca atāttvikam eva caitanyena buddheḥ vāstava sambandha abhāvāt yathā darpaṇam mukha sambandham antareṇa api puruṣa bhramaḥ tadvat iha api 
STPradīpikā_0.4^4	 idam iti viṣaya upadhānam tat ca vāstavam ekaḥ indriya praṇālikayā buddhau jñāna rūpa viṣaya sambandhāt darpaṇasya iva niḥśvāsa abhihatasya malinimā pāramārthikaḥ puruṣa uparāgaḥ viṣaya uparāga māhimnā ca kṛti mat tayā adhyavasāyaḥ ayam eva vyāpāra āveśaḥ atha jñānam eva caitanyam katham anayoḥ bhedaḥ iti ucyate kṛtī adhyavasāya lakṣaṇa vyāpāra viśiṣṭā buddhiḥ indriya praṇālikayā buddheḥ viṣayasya ghaṭa ādeḥ sambandhaḥ vṛtti viśeṣaḥ tat eva jñānam viṣaya uparāgeṇa saha yaḥ puruṣa uparāgasya atāttvikaḥ sambandhaḥ saḥ upalabdhiḥ yathā darpaṇa niṣṭhā malinimā darpaṇa pratibimbita mukhe dṛśyate tathā iti yāvat tathā ghaṭam aham jānāmi iti anuvyavasāya ākhya upalabdhiḥ iti vibhāvanīyam
