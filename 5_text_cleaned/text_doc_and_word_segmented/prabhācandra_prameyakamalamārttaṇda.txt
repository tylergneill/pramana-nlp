PKM_247,24–248,7^2	 pravṛttau vā adhyakṣeṇa eva asya pratipanna tvāt na kiñcid anumānena na api anumānena hetoḥ pakṣa dharmatā avagamam antareṇa anumānasya eva apravṛtteḥ na ca apratipanne dharmiṇi hetoḥ tad sambandha avagamaḥ na api apratipanna pakṣa dharma tvaḥ hetoḥ pratiniyata sādhya pratipatti aṅgam kiñ ca sattā sādhane sarvaḥ hetoḥ asiddha viruddha anaikāntika tva lakṣaṇām trayīm doṣa jātim na ativarttate tathā hi sarvajña sattve sādhye bhāva dharmaḥ hetoḥ abhāva dharmaḥ vā syāt uta ubhaya dharmaḥ vā prathama pakṣe asiddhaḥ bhāve asiddhe tad dharmasya siddhi virodhāt dvitīya pakṣe tu viruddhaḥ bhāve
