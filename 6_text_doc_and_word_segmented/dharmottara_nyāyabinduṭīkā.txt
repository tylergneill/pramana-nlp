NBṬ_1.1^1	samyak jñāna pūrvikā sarva puruṣa artha siddhiḥ iti tat vyutpādyate jayanti jāti vyavasana prabandha prasuti hetoḥ jagataḥ vijetuḥ rāda ādi arāteḥ sugatasya vācaḥ manasta mata anavamāt aghānāḥ samyak jñāna pūrvikā ityādinā asya prakaraṇasya abhidheya prayojanam ucyate dvividham hi prakaraṇa śarīram śabdaḥ arthaḥ ca tatra śabdasya sva abhidheya pratipādanam eva prayojanam na anyat atas tat na nirūpyate abhidheyam tu yadi niṣprayojanam syāt tadā tad pratipattaye śabda sandarbhaḥ api na ārambhaṇīyaḥ syāt yathā kāka danta prayojana abhāvāt na tad parīkṣā ārambhaṇīyā prekṣāvatā 
NBṬ_1.1^2	 tasmāt asya prakaraṇasya ārambhaṇīya tvam darśayatā abhidheya prayojanam anena ucyate yasmāt samyak jñāna pūrvikā sarva puruṣa artha siddhiḥ tasmāt tad pratipattaye idam ārabhyate iti ayam atra vākya arthaḥ atra ca prakaraṇa abhidheyasya samyak jñānasya sarva puruṣa artha siddhi hetu tvam prayojanam uktam asmin ca arthaḥ ucyamāne sambandha prayojana abhidheyāni uktāni bhavanti tathā hi prurṣa arthaḥ apayogi samyak jñānam vyutpādayitavyam anena prakareṇa iti bruvatā samyak jñānam asya śabda sandarbhasya abhidheyam tad vyutpādanam prayojanam prakaraṇam ca idam apāyaḥ vyutpādanasya iti uktam bhavati 
NBṬ_1.1^3	 tasmāt abhidheya bhāga prayojana abhidhāna sāmarthyāt sambandha ādīni uktāni bhavanti na tu idam ekam vākyam sambandham abhidheyam prayojanam ca vaktum sākṣāt samartham ekam tu vadat trayam sāmarthyāt darśayati tatra tat iti abhidheya padam vyutpādyate iti prayojana padam prayojanam ca atra vaktuḥ prakaraṇa karaṇa vyāpārasya cintyate śrotuḥ ca śravaṇa vyāpārasya tathā hi sarve prekṣāvantaḥ pravṛtti prayojanam anviṣya pravartante tataśa ca ācāryeṇa prakaraṇam kim artham kṛtam śrotṛbhiḥ ca kim artham śruyate iti saṃśaye vyutpādanam prayojana abhidhīyate 
NBṬ_1.1^4	 samyak jñānam vyutpadyamānānām ātmānam vyutpādakam kartum prakaraṇam idam kṛtam śiṣyaiḥ ca ācārya prayuktām ātmanaḥ vyutpattim icchadbhiḥ prakaṇam idam śruyate iti prakaraṇa karaṇa śravaṇayoḥ prayojanam vyutpādanam sambandha pradarśana padam tu na vidyate sāmarthyāt eva tu sa pratipattavyaḥ prekṣāvatā hi samyak jñānau avyutpādanāya prakaraṇam idam ārabdhavatā ayam eva upāyo ja anyaḥ iti darśitaḥ eva upāya upeya bhāvaḥ prakaraṇa prayojanayoḥ sambandhaḥ iti nanu ca prakaraṇa śravaṇāt prāk uktāni api abhidheya ādīni pramāṇa abhāvāt prekṣāvadbhiḥ na grahyante 
NBṬ_1.1^5	 tat kim etaiḥ ārambha pradeśe uktaiḥ satyam aśruyate prakaraṇe kathitāni api na niścīyante ukteṣu tu apramāna keśu api abhidheya ādiśu saṃśayaḥ utpadyate saṃśayāt ca pravartante artha saṃsayaḥ api hi pravṛtti aṅgam prekṣāvatām anartha saṃśayaḥ api nivṛtti aṅgam atas eva śāstra kāreṇa eva pūrvam sambandha ādīni yujyante vaktum vyākhyātāṇām hi vacanam krīḍā ādi artham anyathā api sambhāvyate śāstra kṛtām tu prakaraṇ prārambhe na viparīta abhidheya ādi abhidhāne prayojanam utpaśyāmaḥ na api pravṛttim atas teṣu saṃśayaḥ yuktaḥ 
NBṬ_1.1^6	 anukteṣu tu pratipatṛbhiḥ niṣprayojanam abhidheyam sambhāvyeta asya prakaraṇasya kāka danta parīkṣāyāḥ iva aśakya anuṣṭhānam vā jvara hara takṣaka cūḍā ratna alaṅkāra upadeśa vat anabhimatam vā prayojanam mātṛ vivāha krama upadeśa vat atas vā prakaraṇāt laghutaraḥ upāyaḥ prayojanasya anupāyaḥ eva vā prakaraṇam sambhāvyate etāsu ca anartha sambhāvanāsu ekasyām api anartha sambhāvanāyām na prekṣāvantaḥ pravartante abhidheya ādiṣu tu ukteṣu artha sambhāvanāḥ anartha sambhāvana aviruddhāḥ utpadyate tathā prekṣāvantaḥ pravartante iti prekṣāvatām pravṛtti aṅgam artha sambhāvanā kartum sambhandha ādīni abhidhīyante iti sthitam 
NBṬ_1.1^7	 avisaṃvādakam jñānam samyak jñānam loke ca pūrvam upadarśitam artham prāpayan saṃvādakaḥ ucyate tadvat jñānam api pradarśitam artham prāpayat saṃvādakam ucyate pradarśite ca arthe pravartaka tvam eva prāpaka tvam na anyat tathā hi na hi jñānam janayat artham prāpayati api tu arthe puruṣam pravartayat prāpayati artham pravartaka tvam api pravṛtti viṣaya pradarśaka tvam eva na hi puruṣam haṭhāt pravartayitum śaknoti vijñānam atas eva ca artha adhigatiḥ eva pramāna phalam adhigate ca arthe pravartitaḥ puruṣaḥ prāpitaḥ ca arthaḥ 
NBṬ_1.1^8	 tathā ca sati artha adhigamāt samāptaḥ pramāṇa vyāpāraḥ atas eva anadhigata viṣayam pramāṇam yena eva hi jñānena pratham adhigataḥ arthaḥ tena eva pravartitaḥ puruṣaḥ prāpitaḥ ca arthaḥ tatra eva ca atha kim anyena jñānena adhikam kāryam atas adhigata viṣayam apramāṇam tatra yaḥ arthaḥ dṛṣṭa tvena jñātaḥ sa pratyakṣeṇa pravṛtti viṣayīkṛtaḥ yasmāt yasmin arthe pratyakṣasya sākṣātkāri tva vyāpāraḥ vikalpena anugamyate tasya pradarśakam pratyakṣam tasmāt dṛṣṭa tayā jñātaḥ pratyakṣa darśitaḥ anumānam tu liṅga darśanāt niścinvat pravṛtti viṣayam darśayati 
NBṬ_1.1^9	 tathā ca pratyakṣam pratibhāsamānam niyatam artham darśayati anumānam ca liṅga sambaddham niyatam artham darśayati atas eva niyatasya arthasya pradarśake tene te pramāṇe na anyat vijñānam prāptum śakyam artham ādarśayat prāpakam prāpaka tvāt ca pramāṇam ābhyām pramāṇābhyām anyena ca darśitaḥ arthaḥ kaścid atyanta viparyastaḥ yathā marīcikāsu jalam sa ca asattvāt pāptum aśakyaḥ kaścid aniyataḥ bhāva abhāvayoḥ yathā saṃśaya arthaḥ na ca bhāva abhāvābhyām yuktaḥ arthaḥ jagati asti tatas prāptum aśakyaḥ tādṛśaḥ 
NBṬ_1.1^10	 sarveṇa ca aliṅgena vikalpena niyāmakam adṛṣṭvā pravṛttena bhāva abhāvayoḥ aniyataḥ eva arthaḥ darśayitavaḥ sa ca prāptum aśayaḥ tasmāt aśaya prāpaṇam atyanta viparītam bhāva abhāva aniyatam ca artham darśayat apramāṇam anyat jñānam artha kriyā arthibhiḥ ca artha kriyā samartha vastu prāpti nimittam jñānam mṛgyate yat ca taiḥ mṛgyate tat eva śastre vicāryate tatas artha kriyā samartha vastu pradarśakam samyak jñānam yat ca tena pradarśitam tat eva prāpaṇīyam artha adhigama ātmakam hi prāpakam iti uktam tatra pradarśitāt anyat vastu bhinna ākāram bhinna deśam bhinna kālam ca viruddha dharma saṃsargāt dhi adyat vastu 
NBṬ_1.1^11	 deśa kāla ākāra bhedaḥ ca viruddha dharma saṃsargaḥ tasmāt anya ākāra vat vastu grāhi naḥ ākāra antaravati vastuni pramāṇam yathā pīta śaṅkha grāhi śukle śaṅkhe deśa antara stha grāhi ca na deśa antara sthe pramāṇam yathā kuñcikā vivara deśa sthāyām maṇi prabhāyām maṇigrāhi jñānam na apavaraka sthe maṇau kāla antara yukta grāhi ca na kāla antaravati vastuni pramāṇam yathā ardharātre madhyāhna kāla vastu grāhi svapna jñānam na ardharātra kāle vastuni pramāṇam nanu ca deśa niyatam ākāra niyatam ca prāpayitum śakyam yad kālam tu paricchinnam tad kālam na śakyam prāpayitum 
NBṬ_1.1^12	 naḥ ucyate yasmin eva kāle paricchidyate tasmin eva kāle prāpayitavyam iti anyaḥ hi darśana kālaḥ anyaḥ ca prāpti kālaḥ kintu yad kālam paricchinnam tat eva tena prāpaṇīyam abheda adhyavasāyāt ca santāna gatam eka tvam draṣṭavyam iti samyak jñānam pūrvam kāraṇam yasyāḥ sā tathā uktā kāryāt pūrvam bhavat kāraṇam pūrvam uktam kāraṇa śabda upādāne tu puruṣa artha shiddhiḥ sākṣāt kāraṇam gamyeta pūrva śabde tu pūrva mātram dvividham ca samyak jñānam artha kriyā nirbhāsam artha kriyā samarthe ca pravartakam tayoḥ madhya yat pravartakam tat iha parīkṣyate 
NBṬ_1.1^13	 tat ca pūrva mātram na tu sākṣātkāraṇam samyak jñāne hi sati pūrva dṛṣṭa smaraṇam smaraṇāt abhilāṣaḥ abhilāṣāt pravṛttiḥ pravṛtteḥ ca prāptiḥ tatas na sākṣāthetuḥ artha kriyā nirbhāsāt tu yadi api sākṣāt prāptiḥ tathā api tat na paṛīkṣaṇīyam yatra eva hi prekṣāvantaḥ arthinaḥ sa āśaṅkāḥ tat parīkṣyate artha kriyā nirbhāse ca jñāne sati siddhiḥ puruṣa arthaḥ tena tatra na sa āśaṅkāḥ arthinaḥ atas tat na parīkṣaṇīyam tasmāt parīkṣā arham asākṣāt kāraṇam samyak jñānam ādarśayitum kāraṇa śadam parityajya pūrva grahaṇam kṛtam puruṣasya arthaḥ puruṣa arthaḥ 
NBṬ_1.1^14	 arthyate iti arthaḥ kāmyate iti yāvat heyaḥ arthaḥ upādeyaḥ vā heyaḥ hi arthaḥ hāntum iṣyate upādeyaḥ api upādātum na ca heya upādeyābhyām anyaḥ rāśiḥ asti upekṣaṇīyaḥ hi anupādeya tvāt heyaḥ eva tasya siddhiḥ hānam upādānam ca hetu nibandhanā hi siddhiḥ utpattiḥ ucyate jñāna nibandhanā tu shiddhiḥ anuṣṭhānam heyasya ca hānam anuṣṭhānam upādeyasya ca upādānam tatas heya upadeyayoḥ hāna upādāna lakṣaṇa anuṣṭhitiḥ siddhiḥ iti ucyate sarvā ca asau puruṣa artha siddhiḥ ca iti sarva śabdaḥ iha dravya kārtsnye vṛttaḥ na tu prakārakārtsnye 
NBṬ_1.1^15	 tatas na ayam arthaḥ dvi prakārāḥ api siddhiḥ samyak jñāna nibandhanāḥ eva iti api tu ayam arthaḥ yā kācid siddhiḥ sā sarvā kṛtsnāḥ eva asau samyak jñāna nibandhanāḥ iti mithyājñānāt dhi kākatālīya api na asti artha siddhiḥ tathā hi yadi pradarśitam artham prāpayati evam tatas bhavati artha siddhiḥ pradarśitam ca prāpayat samyak jñānam eva pradarśitam ca aprāpayat mithyājñānam aprāpakam ca katham artha siddhi nibandhanam syāt tasmāt yat mithyājñānam na tatas artha siddhiḥ yatas ca artha siddhiḥ tat samyak jñānam eva atas eva samyak jñānam yatnatas vyutpādanīyam 
NBṬ_1.1^16	 yatas tat eva puruṣa artha siddhi nibandhanam tatas yāvat brūyāt yā kācid puruṣa artha siddhiḥ sā samyak jñāna nibandhanāḥ eva iti tāvat uktam sarvā sā samyak jñāna pūrvikāḥ iti iti śabdaḥ tasmāt iti asmin arthe yat tadoḥ ca nityam abhisambandhaḥ tat ayam arthaḥ yasmāt samyak jñāna pūrvikā sarva puruṣa artha siddhiḥ tasmāt tat samyak jñānam vyutpādyate yadi api ca samāse guṇībhūtam samyak jñānam tathā api iha prakaraṇe vyutpādayitavya tvāt pradhānam tatas tasya eva tad śabdena sambandhaḥ vyutpādyate iti vipratipatti nirākaraṇena pratipādyate iti 
NBṬ_1.2	 caturvidhā ca atra vipratipattiḥ saṃkhyā lakṣaṇa gocara phala viṣayāḥ tatra saṃkhyā vipratipattim nirākartum āha dvividham samyak jñānam dvividham iti dvau vidhau prakārau asya iti dvividham saṃkhyā pradarśana dvāreṇa ca vyakti bhedaḥ darśitaḥ bhavati dve eva samyak jñāna vyaktī iti vyakti bhede ca pradarśite prati vyakti niyatam samyak jñānam lakṣaṇam ākhyātum śakyam apradarśite tu vyakti bhede sakala vyakti anuyāyi samyak jñāna lakṣaṇam ekam sa śakyam vaktum tatas lakṣaṇa kathana aṅgam eva saṃkhyā bheda kathanam apradarśita tu vyakti bheda ātmake saṃkhyā bhede lakṣaṇabhdasya darśayitum aśakya tvāt lakṣaṇa nirdeśa aṅga tvāt eva ca prathamam saṃkhyā bheda kathanam 
NBṬ_1.3^1	 kim punar tat dvaividhyam iti āha pratyakṣam anumānan ca iti pratyakṣam iti parigatam āśritam akṣam ati ādayaḥ krānta ādi arthe dvitīyayā iti samāsaḥ prāpta āpannālaṅgati samāseṣu para vat liṅga pratiśedhāt abhideya vat liṅge sati sarva liṅgaḥ pratyakṣa śabdaḥ siddhaḥ ākṣa āśrita tvam ca vyutpatti nimittam śabdasya na tu pravṛtti nimittam anena tu akṣa āśrita tvena eka artha samavetam artha sākṣāt kāri tvam lakṣyate tat eva śabdasya pravṛtti nimittam tatas ca yat kiñcid arthasya sākṣātkāri jñānam tat pratyakṣam ucyate 
NBṬ_1.3^2	 yadi tu akṣāśrtatvam eva pravṛtti nimittam syāt indriya vijñānam eva pratyakṣam ucyeta na mānāsa ādi yathā gacchati gauḥ iti gamana kriyāyām vyutpāditaḥ api go śabdaḥ gamana kriyā upalakṣitam eka artha samavetam go tvam pravṛtti nimittīkaroti tathā ca gacchati agacchati ca gavi go śabdaḥ siddhaḥ bhavati mīyate anena iti mānam karaṇa sādhanena māna śabdena sārūpya lakṣaṇam pramāṇam abhidhīyate liṅga grahaṇa sambandha smaraṇasya paścāt mānam anumānam gṛhīte pakṣa dharme smṛte ca sādhya sādhana sambandhe anumānam pravartate iti paścāt kāla bhāvi ucyate 
NBṬ_1.4^1	 cakāraḥ pratyakṣa anumānayoḥ tulya bala tvam samuccinoti yathā artha avinā bhāvi tvāt artham prāpayat pratyakṣam pramāṇam tadvat artha avinā bhāvi tvāt anumānam api paricchinnam artham prāpayat pramāṇam iti tatra pratyakṣam kalpanā apodham abhrāntam tatra iti saptamī arthe vartamānaḥ nirdhāraṇe vartate tatas ayam vākya arthaḥ tatra tayoḥ pratyakṣa anumānayoḥ iti samudāya nirdeśaḥ pratyakṣam iti eka deśa nirdeśaḥ tatra samudāyāt pratyakṣa tva jāti eka deśasya pṛthak karaṇam nirdhāraṇam tatra pratyakṣam anudya kalpanā apoḍha tvam abhrānta tvam ca vidhīyate yat tat bavatām asmākam artheṣu sākṣātkāri jñānam prasiddham tat kalpanā poḍhā bhra anta tva ayuktam draṣṭavyam 
NBṬ_1.4^2	 na ca etat mantavyam kalpanā poḍha abhra anta tvam ced aprasiddham kim anyat pratyakṣaya jñānasya rūpam avaśiṣyate yat pratyakṣa śabda vācyam srveśām prasiddham tat anuvādena kalpanā poḍhā bhra anta tva vidhiḥ kalpanāyāḥ apoḍham apetam kalpanā apoḍham kalpanā svabhāvena rahitam iti arthaḥ abhrāntam artha kriyā kṣame vastu rūpe aviparyastam ucyate artha kriyā kṣamam ca vastu rūpam sanniveśa upādhi varṇa ātmakam tatra yat na bhrāmyati tat abhra antam etat ca lakṣaṇa dvayam vipratipatti nirāsa artham na tu anumāna nivṛtti artham yatas kalpanā apoḍha grahaṇena eva anumānam nivartitam tatra asati abhra anta grahaṇe gacchat vṛkṣa darśana ādi pratyakṣam kalpanā apoḍha tvāt syāt 
NBṬ_1.4^3	 tatas hi pravṛttena vṛkṣa mātram avāpyate iti saṃvādaka tvāt samyak jñānam kalpanā apoḍha tvāt ca pratyakṣam iti syāt āśaṅkā tad nivṛtti artham abhra anta grahaṇam tat dhi bhrānta tvāt na pratyakṣam trirūpa liṅga ja tvā abhāvāt ca na anumānam na ca pramāṇa antaram asti atas gacchat vṛkṣa darśana ādi mithyājñānam iti uktam bhavati yadi mithyājñānam katham tatas vṛkṣa avāptiḥ iti ced na tatas vṛkṣa avāptiḥ nānā deśa gamī hi vṛkṣaḥ tena paricchinaḥ eka deśa niyataḥ ca vṛkṣaḥ avāpyate tatas yat dṛśaḥ gacchat vṛkṣaḥ dṛṣṭaḥ tad dṛśaḥ na avāpyate yad deśaḥ ca avāpyate sa na dṛṣṭaḥ iti na tasmāt kaścid arthaḥ avāpyate jñāna antarāt eva tu vṛkṣa ādiḥ arthaḥ avāpyate iti evam abhra anta grahaṇam vipratipatti nirāsa artham 
NBṬ_1.4^4	 tathā abhra anta grahaṇena api anumāne nivartate kalpanā apoḍha grahaṇam vipratipatti nirākaraṇa artham abhrāntam hi anumānam sva pratibhāse anarthe artha adhyavasāyena pravṛtta tvāt pratyakṣam tu grāhya rūpe na viparyastam na tu avisaṃvādakam abhra antam iha grahītayam yatas samyak jñānam eva pratyakṣam na anyat tatra samyak jñāna tvāt eva avisaṃvādaka tve labdhe punar avisaṃvādaka grahaṇam niṣprayojanam eva evam hi vākya arthaḥ syāt pratyakṣa ākhyam yat avisaṃvādakam jñānam tat kalpanā apoḍham avisaṃvādakam ca iti na ca anena dvis avi saṃvādaka grahaṇena kiñcid tasmāt grāhye artha kriyā kṣame vastu rūpe yat aviparyastam tat abhra antam iha veditavyam 
NBṬ_1.5^1	 kīdṛśī punar kalpanā iha gṛhyate iti āha abhilāpa saṃsarga yogya pratibhāsā pratītiḥ kalpanā abhlāpaḥ ityādi abhilāpyate anena iti abhilāpaḥ vācakaḥ śabdaḥ abhilāpena saṃsargaḥ abhilāpa saṃsargaḥ ekasmin jñāne abhidheya ākārasya abhidhāna ākāreṇa saha grāhya ākāra tayā milanam tatas yadā ekasmin jñāne abhidheya abhidhānayoḥ ākārau saṃniviṣṭau bhavataḥ tadā saṃsṛṣṭe abhidhāna abhidheye bhavataḥ abhilāpa saṃsargāya yogyaḥ abhidheya ākāra ābhāsaḥ yasyām pratītau sā tathā ukatā tatra kācid pratītiḥ abhilāpa saṃsṛṣṭa ābhāsā bhavati yathā vyutpanna saṅketasya ghaṭa artha kalpanā ghaṭa śabda saṃsṛṣṭa avabhāsā bhavati kācid tu abhilāpena asaṃsṛṣṭāḥ api abhilāpa saṃsarga yogya ābhāsā bhavati yadā bālakasya avyutpanna saṅketasya kalpanā 
NBṬ_1.5^2	 tatra abhilāpa saṃsṛṣṭa ābhāsā kalpanā iti uktau avyutpanna saṅketasya kalpanā na saṃgṛhyeta yogya grahaṇe tu sā api saṃgṛhyate yadi api abhilāpa saṃsṛṣṭa ābhāsā na bhavati tad arha jātasya bālakasya kalpanā abhilāpa saṃsarga yogya pratibhāsā tu bhavati eva yā ca abhilāpa saṃsṛṣṭā sā api yogyā tatas ubhayoḥ api yogya grahaṇena saṃgrahaḥ asati abhilāpa saṃsarge kutas yogyatā avasitiḥ iti ced aniyata pratibhāsa tvāt aniyata pratibhāsa tvam ca pratibhāsa niyama hetoḥ abhāvāt grāhyaḥ hi arthaḥ vijñānam janayan niyata pratibhāsam kuryāt yathā rūpam cakṣuḥ vijñānam janayat niyata pratibhāsam janayati vikalpa vijñānam tu arthāt naḥ utpadyate tatas pratibhāsa niyama hetoḥ abhāvāt aniyata pratibhāsam kutas punar etat vikalpaḥ arthān naḥ utpadyate iti artha sannidhi nirapekṣa tvāt 
NBṬ_1.5^3	 bālaḥ api hi yāvat dṛśyamānam stanam saḥ eva ayam iti pūrva dṛṣṭa tvena na pratyavamṛśati tāvat naḥ uparata ruditaḥ mukham arpayati stane pūrva dṛṣṭa apara dṛṣṭam ca artham ekīkurvat vijñānam asat nihita viṣayam pūrva dṛṣṭa tvasya asat nihita tvāt asat nihita viṣayam ca artha nirapekṣam anapekṣam ca pratibhāsa niyama hetoḥ abhāvāt aniyata pratibhāsam tādṛśam ca abhilāpa saṃsarga yogyam indriya vijñānam tu sannihita artha mātra grāhi tvāt artha sāpekṣam arthasya ca pratibhāsa niyama hetu tvāt niyata pratibhāsam tatas na abhilāpa saṃsarga yogyam atas eva sva lakṣaṇasya api vācya vācaka bhāvam abhyupagamya etat avikalpaka tvam ucyate yadi api hi sva lakṣaṇam eva vācyam vācakam ca bhāvet tathā api abhilāpa saṃsṛṣṭa artha vijñānam sa vikalpakam 
NBṬ_1.5^4	 na ca indriya vijñānam arthena niyamita pratibhāsa tvāt abhilāpa saṃsarga yogya pratibhāsam bhavati iti nirvikalpakam śrotra jñānam tarhi śabda sva lakṣaṇa grāhi śabda sva lakṣaṇam ca kiṇcit vācyam kiñcid vācakam iti abhilāpa saṃsarga yogya pratibhāsam syāt tathā ca sa vikalpakam syāt na eṣa doṣaḥ sati api sva lakṣaṇasya vācya vācaka bhāve saṅketa kāla dṛṣṭa tvena grahyamāṇam sva lakṣaṇam vācyam vācakam ca gṛhītam syāt na ca saṅketa kāla bhāvi darśana viṣaya tvam vastunaḥ samprati asti yathā hi saṅketa kāla bhāvi darśanam adya viruddham tadvat tad viṣaya tvam api arthasya adya na asti tatas pūrva kāla dṛṣṭa tvam apaśyat śrotra jñānam na vācaka bhāva grāhi anena eva nyāyena yogi jñānam api sakala śabda artha avabhāsi tve api saṅketa kāla sṛṣṭa tva agrahaṇāt nirvikalpakam 
NBṬ_1.6^1	 tayā rahitam timira āśu bhra maṇa nauyāna saṃkṣobha ādi anāhita vibhramam jñānam pratyakṣam tayā kalpanayā kalpanā svabhāvena rahitam śūnyam sat jñānam yat abhra antam tat pratyakṣam iti pareṇa sambandhaḥ kapana apoḍha tva abhra anta tve paraspara sāpekṣe pratyakṣa lakṣaṇam na pratyekam iti darśayitum tayā rahitam tat pratyakṣam iti lakṣaṇayoḥ parasparasa apekṣayoḥ pratyakṣa viṣaya tvam darśitam iti timiram akṣanoḥ viplavaḥ indriya gatam idam vibhrama kāraṇam āśu bhramaṇam alāta ādeḥ mandam hi bhramyamāṇe alātā ādau na cakra bhrāntiḥ utpadyate tad artham āśu grahaṇena viśeṣyate bhramaṇam etat ca viṣaya gatam vibhrama kāraṇam nāvā gamanam nauyānam gacchantyām nāvi sthitasya gacchat vṛkṣa ādi bhrāntiḥ utpadyate iti yāna grahaṇam 
NBṬ_1.6^2	 etat ca bāhya āśraya sthitam vibhrama kāraṇam saṃkṣobhaḥ vāta pītta śleṣmaṇām vāta ādiṣu hi kṣobham gateṣu jvalita stambha ādi bhrāntiḥ utpadyate etat ca adhyātma gatam vibhrama kāraṇam sarvaiḥ eva ca vibhrama kāraṇaiḥ indriya viṣaya bāhya ādhyātmika āśraya gataiḥ indriyam eva vikartavyam avikṛte indriye indriya bhrānti ayogāt ete samkṣobha paryantāḥ ādayaḥ yeṣām te tathā uktāḥ ādi grahaṇena kāca kāmalā ādayaḥ indriya sthāḥ gṛhyante āśunayana ānayana ādayaḥ viśaya sthāḥ āśunayana ānayane hi kāryamāne alāte agnibarṇa daṇḍa ābhāsā bhrāntiḥ bhavati hasti yāna ādayaḥ bāhya āśraya sthāḥ gāḍa marma prahāra ādayaḥ ādhyātmika āśraya sthāḥ vibhrama hetavaḥ gṛhyante taiḥ anāhitaḥ vibhramaḥ yasmin tathāvidham jñānam pratyakṣam 
NBṬ_1.7–1.8	 tat eva lakṣaṇam ākhyāya yaiḥ indriyam eva dṛṣṭa kalpitam mānasa prayakṣa lakṣaṇe ca doṣaḥ udbhāvitaḥ sva saṃvedanam ca na abhyupagatam yogi jñānam ca teṣām vipratipatti nirākaraṇa artham prakāra bhedam pratyakṣasya darśayan āha tat caturvidham tat caturvidham iti indriya jñānam idriyasya jñānam indriya jñānam indriya āśritam tat pratyakṣām 
NBṬ_1.9^1	 mānasa pratyakṣe paraiḥ yaḥ doṣaḥ udbhāvitaḥ tam nirākartum mānasa pratyakṣa lakṣaṇam āha sva viṣaya anantara viṣaya sahakāriṇā indriya jñānena samanantara pratyayena janitam tat manaḥ vijñānam svātmīyaḥ viṣayaḥ indirya jñānasya tasya anantaraḥ na vidyate antaram asya iti antaram ca vyavadhānam viśeṣaḥ ca ucyate tatas ca antare pratiṣiddhe samānajatīyaḥ dvitīya kṣaṇa bhāvi upādeya kṣaṇa idriya vijñāna viṣayasya gṛhyate sa sahakārī yasya indriya jñānasya tat tathā uktam dvividhaḥ ca sahakārī paraspara upakārī eka kārya upakārī ca iha ca kṣaṇike vastuni atiśaya ādhāna ayogāt eka kārya kāri tvena sahakārī gṛhyate viṣaya vijñānābhyām hi manaḥ vijñānam ekam kriyate yatas tat anayoḥ paraspara sahakāri tvam īdṛśena indriya vijñānena ālambana bhūtena api yogi jñānam janyate tat nirāsa artham samanantara pratyaya grahaṇam kṛtam 
NBṬ_1.9^2	 samaḥ ca asau jñāna tvena anantaraḥ ca asau avyavahita tatvena sa ca asau pratyayaḥ ca hetu tvāt samanantara pratyayaḥ tena janitam tat anena eka santāna antarbhūtayoḥ eva indriya jñāna manaḥ vijñānayoḥ janya janaka bhāve manaḥ vijñānam pratyakṣam iti uktam bhavati tatas yogi jñānam para santāna vartim nirāstam yadā ca indriya vijñānāt anyaḥ viṣayaḥ manaḥ vijñānasya tadā gṛhita grahaṇāt āsañjitaḥ aprāmāṇya doṣaḥ nirastaḥ yadā ca indriya viṣaya upādeya bhūtaḥ kṣaṇaḥ gṛhītaḥ tadā indriya jñānena agṛhītasya viṣaya antarasya grahaṇāt andha badhira ādi a bhāva doṣa prasaṅgaḥ nirastaḥ etat ca manaḥ vijñānam uparata vyāpāre cakṣuṣi pratyakṣam iṣyate vyāpāravati tu cakṣuṣi yat rūpa jñānam tat sarvam cakṣuḥ āśritam eva itarathā cakṣuḥ āśrita tva anupapattiḥ kasyacid api vijñānasya etat ca siddhānta prasiddham mānasam pratyakṣam na tu asya prasādhakam asti pramāṇam evaṃjatīyakam tat yadi syāt na kaścid doṣaḥ syāt iti vaktum lakṣaṇam ākhyātam asya iti 
NBṬ_1.10^1	 sva saṃvedam ākhyātum āha sarva citta caittānām ātma saṃvedanam sarva cittā etya ādi cittam artha mātra grāhi caittāḥ viśesa avasthā grāhiṇaḥ sakhā ādayaḥ sarve ca te citta caittāḥ ca sarva citta caittāḥ sukha ādayaḥ eva sphuṭa anubhava tvāt sva saṃviditāḥ na anyā citta avasthā iti etat āśaṅkā nivṛtti artham sarva grahaṇam kṛtam na asti sā kācid citta avasthā yasyām ātmanaḥ saṃvedanam na pratyakṣam syāt yena hi rūpeṇa ātmā vedyate tad rūpam ātma samvedanam pratyakṣam iha ca rūpa ādau vastuni dṛśyamāne āntaraḥ sukha ādi ākāraḥ tulya kālam saṃvedyate 
NBṬ_1.10^2	 na ca gṛhyamāṇa ākāraḥ nīla ādiḥ sāta rūpaḥ vedyate iti śakyam vaktum yatas nīla ādiḥ sāta rūpeṇa anubhūyate iti na niścīyate yadi hi sāta rūpaḥ ayam nīla ādiḥ anubhūyate iti niścīyeta syāt tadā tasya sātā ādi rūpa tvam yasmin rūpe pratyakṣasya sākṣātkāri tva vyāpāraḥ vikalpena anugamyate tat pratyakṣam na ca nīlasya sāta rūpa tvam anugamyate tasmāt sātā nīla ādi arthāt anyat eva sātam anubhūyate nīla anubhava kāle tat ca jñānam eva tatas asti jñāna anubhavaḥ tat ca jñāna rūpa vedanam ātmanaḥ sākṣātkāri nirvikalapakam abhra antam ca tatas pratyakṣam 
NBṬ_1.11^1	 yogi pratyakṣam vyākhyātum āha bhūta artha bhāvanā prakarṣa paryanta jam yogi jñānam ca iti bhūtaḥ sadbhūtaḥ arthaḥ pramāṇena dṛṣṭaḥ ca sadbhūtaḥ yathā catvāri ārya satyāni bhūtasya bhāvanā punar punar cetasi viniveśanam bhāvanāyāḥ prakrṣaḥ bhāvyamāna artha ābhāsasya jñānasya sphuṭa ābha tva ārambhaḥ prakrṣasya paryantaḥ yadā sphuṭa ābha tvam īṣat asampūrṇam bhavati yāvat dhi sphuṭa ābha tvam apripūrṇam tāvat tasya prakarṣa gamanam sampūrṇam tu yadā tadā na asti prakarṣa gatiḥ tatas sampūrṇa avasthāyāḥ prāc tani avasthā sphuṭa ābha tva prakarṣa paryantaḥ ucyate tasmāt paryantāt yat jātam bhāvyamānasya arthasya sannihitasya iva sphuṭatara ākāra grāhi jñānam yoginaḥ pratyakṣam tat iha sphuṭa ābha tva ārambha avasthāḥ bhāvanā prakarṣaḥ abhraka vyavahitam iva yadā bhāvyamānam vastu paśyati sā prakarṣa paryanta avasthā 
NBṬ_1.11^2	 kara tala āmalaka vat bhāvyamānasya arthasya yat darśanam tat yoginaḥ pratyakṣam tat dhi sphuṭa ābham sphuṭa ābha tvāt eva ca nirvikalpakam vikalpa vijñānam hi saṅketa kāla dṛṣṭa tvena vastu gṛhṇat śabda saṃsarga yogyam gṛhṇīyāt saṅketa kāla dṛṣṭa tvam ca saṅketa kāla utpanna jñāna viṣaya tvam yathā ca pūrva utpannam vinaṣṭam jñānam samprati asat tadvat pūrva vinaṣṭa jñāna viṣaya tvam api samprati na asti vastunaḥ tat asat rūpam vastunaḥ gṛhṇat asannihita artha grāhivāt asphuṭa ābham vikalpakam tatas sphuṭa ābha tvāt nirvikalpakam pramāṇa śuddha artha grāhi tvāt ca saṃvādakam atas pratyakṣam itara pratyakṣa vat yogaḥ samādhiḥ sa yasya asti sa yogī tasya jñānam pratyakṣam iti śabdaḥ parisamāpti vacanaḥ iyat eva pratyakṣam iti 
NBṬ_1.12	 tat evam pratyakṣasya kalpanā apoḍha tva abhra anta tva yuktasye prakāra bhedam pratipādya viṣaya vipratipattim nirākartum āha tasya viṣayaḥ sva lakṣaṇam tasya ityādi tasya caturvidhasya pratyakṣasya viṣayaḥ boddhavyaḥ sva lakṣaṇam svam asādhāraṇam lakṣaṇam tattvam sva lakṣaṇam vastunaḥ hi asādhāraṇam ca tattvam asti sāmānyam ca tatra yat asādhāraṇam tat pratyakṣasya grāhyam dvividhaḥ hi viṣayaḥ pramāṇasya grāhyaḥ ca yat ākāram utpadyate prāpaṇīyaḥ ca yam adhyavasyati anyaḥ hi grāhyaḥ anyaḥ ca adhyavaseyaḥ pratyakṣasya hi kṣaṇaḥ ekaḥ grāhyaḥ adhyavaseyaḥ tu pratyakṣa bala utpannena niścayena santānaḥ eva santānaḥ eva ca pratyakṣasya prāpaṇīyaḥ kṣaṇasya prāpayitum aśakya tvāt tathā anumānam api sva pratibhāse anarthe artha adhyavasāyena pravṛtteḥ anartha grāhi sa punar āropitaḥ arthaḥ gṛhyamāṇaḥ sva lakṣaṇa tvena avasīyate yatas tatas sva lakṣaṇam avasitam pravṛtti viṣayaḥ anumānasya anarthaḥ tu grāhyaḥ tat atra pramāṇasya grāhyam viṣayam darśayatā pratyakṣasya sva lakṣaṇam viṣayaḥ uktaḥ 
NBṬ_1.13	 kaḥ punar asau viṣayaḥ jñānasya yaḥ sva lakṣaṇam pratipattavyaḥ iti āha yasya arthasya saṃnidhāna asaṃnidhānābhyām jñāna pratibhāsa bhedaḥ tat sva lakṣaṇam yasya arthasya ityādi artha śabdaḥ viṣaya paryāyaḥ yasya jñāna viṣayasya saṃnidhānam nikaṭa deśa avasthānam asaṃnidhānam dūra deśa avasthānam tasmāt saṃnidhāna asaṃnidhānāt ca jñāna pratibhāsasya grāhya ākārasya bhedaḥ sphuṭa tva asphuṭa tvābhyām yaḥ hi jñāna viṣayaḥ saṃnihitaḥ san sphuṭa ābhāsam jñānasya karoti asaṃnihitaḥ tu yogya deśa sthaḥ eva asphuṭam karoti tat sva lakṣaṇam sarvaṇi eva hi vastuni dūrāt asphuṭāni dṛśyante samīṣe sphuṭāni tāni eva sva lakṣaṇāni 
NBṬ_1.14	 kasmāt punar pratyakṣa viṣayaḥ eva sva lakṣaṇam tathā hi vikalpa viṣayaḥ api vahniḥ dṛśya ātmakaḥ eva avasīyate iti āha tat eva paramārtha sat tat eva paramārtha sat ityādi paramaḥ arthaḥ akṛtrimam anāropitam rūpam tena asti iti parama artha sat yaḥ eva arthaḥ sannidhāna āsannidhānābhyām sphuṭam asphuṭam ca pratibhāsam karoti paramārtha san saḥ eva sa ca pratyakṣasya viṣayaḥ yatas tasmāt tat eva sva lakṣaṇam 
NBṬ_1.15	 kasmāt punar tat eva parama artha sat iti āha artha kriyā sāmarthya lakṣaṇa tvāt vastunaḥ arthyate iti arthaḥ heyaḥ upādeyaḥ ca heyaḥ hi hātum iṣyate upādeyaḥ ca upādātum arthasya prayojanasya kriyā niśpattiḥ tasyām sāmarthyam śaktiḥ tat eva lakṣaṇam rūpam yasya vastunaḥ tat artha kriyā sāmarthya lakṣaṇam tasya bhāvaḥ tasmāt vastu śabdaḥ parma artha paryāyaḥ tat ayam arthaḥ yasmāt artha kriyā samartham parma artha sad ucyate sannidhāna asannidhānābhyām ca jñāna pratibhāsasya bhedakaḥ arthaḥ artha kriyā samarthaḥ tasmāt saḥ eva paramārtha san tatas eva hi pratyakṣa viṣayāt artha kriyā prāpyate na vikalpa viṣayāt atas eva yadi api vikalpa viṣayaḥ dṛśyaḥ iva avasīyate tathā api na sa dṛśyaḥ eva tatas artha kriyāya abhāvāt dṛśyāt ca bhāvāt atas tat eva sva lakṣaṇam na vikalpa viṣayaḥ 
NBṬ_1.16	 anyat sāmānya lakṣaṇam anyat ityādi etasmāt sva lakṣaṇāt yat anyat sva lakṣaṇam yaḥ na bhavati jñāna viṣayaḥ tat sāmānya lakṣaṇam vikalpa jñānena avasīyamānaḥ hi arthaḥ sannidhāna āsannidhānābhyām jñāna pratibhāsam na bhinatti tathā hi āropyamāṇaḥ vahniḥ āropāt asti āropāt ca dūra sthaḥ nikaṭa sthaḥ ca tasya samāropitasya sannidhāna āsannidhānāt ca jñāna pratibhāsasya na bhedaḥ sphuṭa tvena asphuṭa tvena vā tatas sva lakṣaṇāt anyaḥ ucyate sāmānyena lakṣaṇam sāmānya lakṣaṇam sādhāraṇam rūpam iti arthaḥ samāropyamāṇam hi rūpam sa kalvahni sādhāraṇam tatas tat sāmāṇi alakṣaṇam 
NBṬ_1.17–1.18	 tat ca anumānyasya grāhyam darśayitum āha saḥ anumānasya viṣayaḥ saḥ anumānasya viṣayaḥ grāhya rūpaḥ sarva nāmnaḥ abhidheya vat liṅga parigrahaḥ sāmānya lakṣaṇam anumānasya viṣayam vyākhyātu kāmena ayam sva lakṣaṇa svarūpa ākhyāna granthaḥ āvartanīyaḥ syāt tatas lāghava artham pratyakṣa paricchedaḥ eva anumāna viṣayaḥ uktaḥ viṣaya vipratipattim nirākṛtya phala vipratipattim nirākarum āha tat eva ca pratyakṣam jñānam pramāṇa phalam tat eva iti yat eva anantaram uktam pratyakṣam jñānam tat eva pramānasya phalam 
NBṬ_1.19	 katham pramāṇa phalam iti āha artha pratīti rūpa tvāt arthasya pratītiḥ avagamaḥ sā eva rūpam yasya pratyakṣa jñānasya tat artha pratīti rūpam tasya bhāvaḥ tasmāt etat uktam bhavati prāpakam jñānam pramāṇam prāpaṇa śaktiḥ ca na kevalāt arthāvinābhavi tvāt bhavati bīja ādi avinābhāvinaḥ api aṅkura ādeḥ aprāpaka tvāt tasmāt prāpyāt arthāt utpattau api asya jñānasya asti kaścid avaśya kartavyaḥ prāpaka vyāpāraḥ yena kṛtena arthaḥ prāpitaḥ bhavati saḥ eva ca pramāṇa phalam yat anuṣṭhānāt prāpakam bhavati jñānam uktam ca pūraḥ tāt pravṛtti viṣaya pradarśanam eva prāpakasya prāpaka vyāpāraḥ nāma tat eva ca pratyakṣam artha pratīti rūpam artha pradarśana rūpam atas tat eva pramāṇa phalam 
NBṬ_1.20	 yadi tarhi jñānam pramiti rūpa tvāt pramāna phalam kim tarhi pramāṇam iti āha artha sārūpyam asya pramāṇam arthena saha yat sārūpyam sādṛśyam asya jñānasya tat pramāṇam iha yasmāt viṣayāt vijñānam udeti tad viṣaya sadṛśam tat bhavati yathā nīlāt utpadyamānam nīla sadṛśam tat ca sārūpyam sādṛśyam ākāraḥ iti ābhāsaḥ iti api vyapadiśyate 
NBṬ_1.21^1	 nanu ca jñānāt avyatiriktam sādṛśyam tathā ca sati tat eva jñānam pramāṇam tat eva ca pramāṇa phalam na ca ekam vastu sādhyam sādhanam ca upapadyate tat katham sārūpyam pramāṇam iti āha tad vaśāt artha pratīti siddheḥ iti tad vaśāt iti tat iti sārūpyam tasya vaśāt sārūpya sāmarthyāt arthasya pratītiḥ avabodhaḥ tasyāḥ siddhiḥ tad siddheḥ kāraṇāt arthasya pratīti rūpam pratyakṣam vijñānam sārūpya vaśāt siddhyati pratītam bhavati iti arthaḥ nīla nirbhāsam hi vijñānam yatas tasmāt nīlasya pratītiḥ avasīyate yebhyaḥ hi cakṣuḥ ādibhyaḥ vijñānam utpadyate na tad vaśāt tat jñānam nīlasya saṃvedanam śakyate avasthāpayitum nīla sadṛśam tu anubhūyamānam nīlasya saṃvedanam avasthāpyate 
NBṬ_1.21^2	 na ca atra janya janaka bhāva nibandhanaḥ sādhya sādhana bhāvaḥ yena ekasmin vastuni virodhaḥ syāt api tu vyavasthāpya vyavasthāpana bhāvena tatas ekasya vastunaḥ kiñcid rūpam pramāṇam kiñcid pramāṇa phalam na virudhyate vyavasthāpana hetuḥ hi sārūpyam tasya jñānasya vyavasthāpyam ca nīla saṃvedana rūpam vyavasthāpya vyavasthāpaka bhāvaḥ api katham ekasya jñānasya iti ced ucyate nīla sadṛśam anubhūya tat vijñānam yatas nīlasya grāhakam avasthāpyate niścaya pratyayena tasmāt sārūpyam anubhūtam vyavasthāpana hetuḥ niścaya pratyayena ca tat jñānam nīla saṃvedanam avasthāpyamānam vyavasthāpyam tasmāt asārūpya vyāvṛttyā sārūpyam jñānasya vyavasthāpana hetuḥ 
NBṬ_1.21^3	 anīla bodha vyāvṛttyā ca nīla bodha rūpa tvam vyavasthāpyam vyavasthāpakaḥ ca vikalpa pratyayaḥ pratyakṣa bala utpannaḥ draṣṭavyaḥ na tu nirvikalpa tvāt pratyakṣam eva nīla bodha rūpa tvena ātmanam avasthāpayitum śaknoti niścaya pratyayena avyavasthāpitam sat api nīla bodha rūpam nijñānam asat kalpam eva tasmāt niścayena nīla bodha rūpam vyavasthāpitam vijñānam nīla bodha ātmanā sat bhavati tasmāt adhyavasāyam kurvat eva pratyakṣam pramāṇam bhavati akṛte tu adhyavasāye nīla bodha rūpa tvena ayavasthāpitam bhavati vijñānam tathā ca pramāṇa phalam artha adhigama rūpam aniṣpannam atas sādhakatama tva abhāvāt pramāṇam eva syāt jñānam 
NBṬ_1.21^4	 janitena tu adhyavasāyena sārūpya vaśāt nīla bodha rūpe jñāne vyavasthāpyamāne sārūpyam vyavasthāpana hetu tvāt pramāṇam siddham bhavati yadi evam adhyavasāya sahitam eva pratyakṣam pramāṇam syāt na kevalam iti ced na etat evam yasmāt pratyakṣa bala utpannena adhyavasāyena dṛṣṭa tvena arthaḥ avasīyate na utprekṣita tvena darśanān ca artha sākṣāt karaṇa ākhyam pratyakṣa vyāpāraḥ utprekṣaṇam tu vikalpa vyāpāraḥ tathā hi parokṣam artham vikalpayantaḥ utprekṣāmahe na tu paśyāmaḥ iti utprekṣa ātmakam vikalpa vyāpāram anubhavāt adhyavasyanti tasmāt sva vyāpāram tiraskṛtya pratyakṣa vyāpāram ādarśayati yatra arthe pratyakṣa pūrvakaḥ adhyavasāyaḥ tatra pratyakṣam kevalam eva pramāṇam iti 
NBṬ_2.1	 evam pratyakṣam vyākhyāya anumānam vyākhyātum āha anumānam dvividhāḥ anumānam dvividhāḥ dvi prakāram atha anumāna lakṣaṇe vaktavye kim akasmāt prakāra bhedaḥ kathyate ucyate para artha anumānam śabda ātmakam sva artha anumānam tu jñāna ātmakam tayoḥ atyanta bhedāt naḥ ekam lakṣaṇam asti tatas tayoh pratiniyatam lakṣaṇam ākhyātum prakāra bhedaḥ kathyate prakāra bhedaḥ hi vyakti bhedaḥ vyakti bhede ca kathite prativyakti niyatam lakṣaṇam śakyate vaktum na anyathā tatas lakṣaṇa nirdeśa aṅgam eva prakāra bheda kathanam aśakyatām ca prakāra bheda kathanam antareṇa lakṣaṇa nirdeśasya jñātvā prāk prakāra bhedaḥ kathyate iti 
NBṬ_2.2–2.3	 kim punar tat dvaividhyam iti āha sva artham para artham ca svasmāyidam sva artham yena svayam pratipadyate tat sva artham para smāyidam para artham yena param pratipādayati tat prarārtham tatra sva artham tri rūpāt liṅgāt yat anumeye jñānam tat anumānam tatra tayoḥ sva artha para artha anumānayoḥ madhye sva artham jñānam kim viśiṣṭam iti āha trirūpāt iti trīṇi rūpāṇi yasya vykṣyamāṇa lakṣaṇāni tat trirūpam liṅgyate gamyate anena artham iti liṅgam tasmāt tri rūpāt liṅgāt yat jātam jñānam iti etat hetu dvāreṇa viśeṣaṇam tat trirūpāt ca liṅgāt trirūpa liṅga ālambhanam api utpadyate iti viśinaṣṭi anumeyaḥ iti etat ca viṣaya dvāreṇa viśeṣaṇam tri rūpāt liṅgāt yat utpannam anumeya ālambhanam jñānam tat sva artham anumānam iti 
NBṬ_2.4	 lakṣaṇa viprattim nirākṛtya phala vipratipattim nirākartum āha pramāṇa phala vyavasthā atra api pratyakṣa vat pramāṇasya yat phalam tasya vā vyavasthā sā atra anumāne api pratyakṣaḥ iva pratyakṣa vat veditavyā yathā hi nīla sarūpam pratyakṣam anubhūyamānam nīla bodha rūpam avasthāpyate tena nīla sārūpyam vyavasthāpana hetuḥ pramāṇam nīla bodha rūpam tu vyavasthāpyamānam pramāṇa phalam tadvat anumānam nīla ākāram utpadyamānam nīla bodha rūpam avasthāpyate tena nīla sārūpyam asya pramāṇam nīla vikalpana rūpam tu asya pramāṇa phalam sārūpya vaṣāt dhi tat nīla pratīti rūpam siddhyate na anyathā iti 
NBṬ_2.5^1	 evam hi saṃkhyā lakṣaṇa phala vipratipattayaḥ pratyakṣa paricchede tu gocara vipratipattim nirākṛtāḥ lakṣaṇa nirdeśa prasaṅgena tu trirūpam liṅgam prastutam tat eva vyākhyātum āha trairūpyam punar liṅgasya anumeye sattvam eva sa pakṣaḥ eva sattvam asapakṣe ca asattvam eva niścitam trairūpyam ityādi liṅgasya yat trairūpyam yāni trīṇi rūpāṇi tat idam ucyate iti śeṣaḥ kim punar tat trairūpyam iti āha anumeyam vakṣyamāṇa lakṣaṇam tasmin liṅgasya sattvam eva niścitam ekam rūpam yadi api ca atra niścita grahaṇam na kṛtam tathā api ante kṛtam prakrāntayoḥ dvayoḥ api rūpayoḥ apekṣaṇīyam yatas ca yogya tayā liṅgam parokṣa jñānasya nimittam yathā bījam aṅkursya adṛṣṭāt dhūmāt agneḥ apratipatteḥ na api sva viṣaya jñāna apekṣam parokṣa artha prakāśanam yathā pradīpaḥ ghaṭa ādeḥ dṛṣṭāt api aniścita sambandhāt apratipatteḥ 
NBṬ_2.5^2	 tasmāt parokṣa artha anantarīyaka tayā niścayanam eva laṅgasya parokṣa artha pratipādana vyāpāraḥ na aparaḥ kaścid atas anvaya vyatireka pakṣa dharma tva niścayaḥ liṅga vyāpāra ātmaka tvāt avaśya kartavyaḥ iti sarveṣu rūpeṣu niścita grahaṇam apekṣaṇīyam tatra sattva vacanena asiddham cākṣuṣa tva ādi nirastam eva kāreṇa pakṣa eka deśa asiddhaḥ nirastaḥ yathā cetāḥ taravaḥ svāpāt iti pakṣīkṛteṣu taruṣu patra saṅkoca lakṣaṇaḥ svāpaḥ eka deśe na siddhaḥ na hi sarve vṛkṣāḥ rātrau patra saṅkoca bhājaḥ kintu kecid eva sattva vacanasya paścāt kṛtena eva kāreṇa asādhāraṇaḥ dharmi nirastaḥ yadi hi anumeyaḥ eva sattvam iti kuryāt śrāvaṇ tvam eva hetuḥ syāt niścita grahaṇena sandigdha asiddhaḥ sarvaḥ nirastaḥ sa pakṣaḥ vakṣyamāṇa lakṣaṇaḥ tasmin eva sattvam niścitam iti dvitīyam rūpam iha api sattva grahaṇena viruddhaḥ rirastaḥ na hi na asti sa pakṣe eva kāreṇa sādhāraṇā anaikāntikaḥ 
NBṬ_2.5^3	 sa hi na sa pakṣaḥ eva vartate kintu ubhayatra api sattva grahaṇāt pūrva avadhāraṇa vacanena sa pakṣa avyāpi sattākasya api prayatna ānantarīyakasya hetu tvam kathitam paścāt avadhāraṇe tu ayam arthaḥ syāt sa pakṣe sattvam eva yasya sa hetuḥ iti prayatna ānantarīyaka tvam na hetuḥ syāt niścita grahaṇena sandigdha anvayaḥ anaikāntikaḥ nirastaḥ yathā sarvajñaḥ kaścid vaktṛ tvāt vaktṛ tvam hi sa pakṣe sandighdam asapakṣa vyakṣyamāṇa lakṣaṇaḥ tasmin asattvam eva niścitam tṛtīyam rūpam tatra asattva grahaṇena viruddhasya nirāsaḥ viruddhaḥ hi vipakṣe asti eva kāreṇa sādhāraṇasya vipakṣa eka deśa vṛtteḥ nirāsaḥ prayatna anantarīyaka tve sādhye hi anitya tvam vipakṣa eka deśe vidyut ādau asti ākāśa ādau na asti tatas niyamena asya nirāsaḥ asattva śabdāt dhi pūrvasmin avadhāraṇe ayam arthaḥ syāt vipakṣaḥ eva yaḥ na asti sa hetuḥ 
NBṬ_2.5^4	 tathā ca prayatna ānantarīyaka tvam sa pakṣe api sarvatra na asti tatas na hetuḥ syāt tatas pūrvam na kṛtam niścita grahaṇena sandigdha vipakṣa vyāvṛttikaḥ anaikāntikaḥ nirastaḥ nanu ca sa pakṣaḥ eva sattvam iti ukte vipakṣe asattvam eva iti gamyate eva tat kim artham punar ubhayoḥ upādānam kṛtam ucyate anvayaḥ vyatirekaḥ vā niyamavān eva prayoktavyaḥ na anyathā iti darśayitum dvayoḥ api upādānam kṛtam aniyate hi dvayoḥ api prayoge ayam arthaḥ syāt sa pakṣe yaḥ asti vipakṣe ca yaḥ na asti sa hetuḥ iti tathā ca sati sa śyāmaḥ tad putra tvāt dṛśyamāna putra vat iti tad putra tvam hetuḥ syāt tasmāt niyamavatoḥ eva anvaya vyatirekayoḥ prayogaḥ kartavyaḥ yena prtibandhaḥ gamyeta sādhanasya sādhyena niyamavatoḥ ca prayoge avaśya kartavye dvayoḥ ekaḥ eva prayoktavyaḥ na dvau iti niyamavān eva anvayaḥ vyatirekaḥ vā prayoktavyaḥ iti śikṣaṇa artham dvayoḥ upādānam iti 
NBṬ_2.6	 trairūpya kathana prasaṅgena anumeyaḥ sa pakṣaḥ vipakṣaḥ ca uktaḥ teṣām lakṣaṇam vaktavyam tatra kaḥ anumeyaḥ iti āha anumeyaḥ atra jijñāsita viśeṣaḥ dharmī anumeyaḥ atra ityādi atra hetu lakṣaṇe niścetavye dharmī anumeyaḥ anyatra tu sādhya pratipatti kāle samudāyaḥ anumeyaḥ vyāpti niścaya kāle tu dharmaḥ anumeyaḥ iti darśayitum atra grahanam jijñāsitaḥ jñātum iṣṭaḥ viśeṣaḥ dharmaḥ yasya dharmiṇaḥ sa tathā uktaḥ 
NBṬ_2.7	 kaḥ sa pakṣaḥ sādhya dharma sāmānyena samānaḥ arthaḥ sa pakṣaḥ samānaḥ arthaḥ sa pakṣaḥ samānaḥ sadśas yaḥ arthaḥ pakṣeṇa sa pakṣaḥ uktaḥ upacārāt samāna śabdena viśiṣyate samānaḥ pakṣaḥ sa pakṣaḥ samānasya ca sa śabdāt eṣaḥ syāt etat kim tat pakṣa sa pakṣayoḥ sāmānyam yena samānaḥ sa pakṣaḥ pakṣena iti āha sādhya dharma sāmānyena iti sādhyaḥ ca asau asiddha tvāt dharmaḥ ca para āśrita tvāt sādhya dharmaḥ na ca viśeṣaḥ sādhyaḥ api tu sāmānyam atas iha samānyam sādhyam uktam sādhya dharmaḥ ca asau sāmānyam ca iti sādhya dharma sāmānyena samānaḥ pakṣeṇa sa pakṣaḥ iti arthaḥ 
NBṬ_2.8–2.9	 kaḥ asapakṣaḥ iti āha na sa pakṣaḥ asapakṣaḥ na sa pakṣaḥ asapakṣaḥ sa pakṣaḥ yaḥ na bhavati saḥ asapakṣaḥ kaḥ ca sa pakṣaḥ na bhavati tatas anyaḥ tad viruddhaḥ tad abhāvaḥ ca iti tatas sapkṣāt anyaḥ tena ca viruddhaḥ tasya ca sa paksayaḥ abhāvaḥ sa pakṣāt anya tvam tat viruddha tvam ca na tāvat pratyetum śakyam tāvat sa pakṣa svabhāva abhāvaḥ na vijñātaḥ tasmāt anya tva viruddha tva pratīti sāmarthyāt sa pakṣa abhāva rūpau pratītau anya viruddhau 
NBṬ_2.10–2.11	 tatas abhāvaḥ sākṣāt sa pakṣa abhāva rūpaḥ pratīyate anya viruddhau tu sāmarthyāt abhāva rūpau pratīyete tatas trayāṇām api asapakṣa tvam trirūpāṇi ca trīṇi eva liṅgāni uktena trairūpyeṇa trirūpāṇi ca trīṇi eva liṅgāni iti cakāraḥ vyaktavyāntara samuccaya arthaḥ trairūpyam ādau pṛṣṭam trirūpāṇi ca liṅgāni pareṇa tatra trairūpyam uktam trirūpāṇi ca ucyante  triṇi eva trirūpāṇi liṅgāni trayaḥ trirūpa liṅga prakārāḥ iti arthaḥ kāni punar tāni iti āha anupalabdhiḥ svabhāvaḥ kāryam ca iti pratiṣedhyasya sādhyasya anupalabdhiḥ trirūpāḥ vidheyasya sādhyasya svabhāvaḥ ca trirūpaḥ kārya ca 
NBṬ_2.12^1	 anupalabdhim udāhartum āha tatra anupalabdhiḥ yathā na pradeśa viśeṣe kvacid ghaṭaḥ upalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāptasya anupalabdheḥ iti yathā ityādi yathā iti upapradarśana artham yathā iyam anupalabdhiḥ tathā anyāḥ api na tu iyam eva iti arthaḥ pradeśaḥ eka deśaḥ viśisyate iti viśeṣaḥ pratipattṛ pratyakṣaḥ tādṛśaḥ ca na sarvaḥ pradeśaḥ tat āha kvacid iti pratipattṛ pratyakṣe kvacid eva pradeśaḥ iti dharmī na ghaṭaḥ iti sādhyam upalabdhiḥ jñānam tasyāḥ lakṣaṇam janikā sāmagrī tathā hi upalabdhiḥ lakṣyate tad prāptaḥ arthaḥ janakatvena sāmagrī antarbhāvāt upalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāptaḥ dṛśyaḥ iti arthaḥ tasya anupalabhdeḥ iti ayam hetuḥ atha yaḥ yatra na asti sa katham tatra dṛśyaḥ dṛśya tva samāropaṭ asan api dṛśyaḥ ucyate yaḥ ca evam sambhāvyate yadi asau atra bhavet dṛśya bhavet iti sa tatra avidyamānaḥ api dṛśyaḥ samāropyaḥ kaḥ ca evam sambhāvyaḥ 
NBṬ_2.12^2	 yasya samagrāṇi su a ālambhana darśana kāraṇāni bhavanti kadā ca tāni samgrāṇi gamyate yadā eka jñāna saṃsargi vaḥ tu antara upalambhaḥ eka aindriya jñāna grāhyam locana ādi praṇidhāna abhimukham vastu dvayam anyonya apekeṣam eka jñāna saṃsargi kathyate tayoḥ hi satoḥ na eka niyatā bhavati pratipattiḥ yogyatāyāḥ dvayoḥ api viśiṣṭa tvāt tasmāt eka jñāna saṃsargiṇi dṛśyamāne sati ekasmin itarat samagra darśana sāmagrīkam yadi bhavet dṛṣyam eva bhavet iti sambhāvitam dṛśya tvam āropyate tasya anupalambhaḥ dṛśya anupalambhaḥ tasmāt saḥ eva ghaṭa vivikta pradeśaḥ tad ālambhanam ca jñānam dṛśya anupalambha niścaya hetu tvāt drśyā anupalambhaḥ ucyate yāvat hi eka jñāna saṃsargi vastu na niścitam tat jñānam ca na tāvat dṛśya anupalambha niścayaḥ tatas vastu anupalambhaḥ ucyate tat jñānam ca darśana nivṛtti mātram tu svayam aniścita tvāt agamakam tatas dṛśya ghaṭa rahitaḥ pradeśaḥ tat jñānam ca vacana sāmarthyāt eva dṛśya anupalambha rūpam uktam draṣṭavyam 
NBṬ_2.13	 kā punar upalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāptiḥ iti āha upalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāptiḥ upalambha pratyaya antarasākalyam svabhāva viśeṣaḥ ca upalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāptiḥ upalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāptavam ghātasya upalambha pratyaya antarasākalyam iti jñānasya ghaṭaḥ api janakaḥ anye ca cakṣuḥ ādayaḥ ghaṭāt dṛśyāt anye hetavaḥ pratyaya antarāṇi teṣām sākalyam sannidhiḥ svabhāvaḥ eva viśiṣyate tana nyasāt iti viśeṣa viśiṣtaḥ iti arthaḥ tat ayam viśiṣṭaḥ svabhāvaḥ pratyaya antara sākalyam ca etat dvayam upalabdhi lakṣana prāpta tvam ghaṭa ādeḥ draṣṭavyam 
NBṬ_2.14	 kīdṛśaḥ svabhāva viśeṣaḥ iti āha yaḥ svabhāvaḥ satsu anyeṣu upalambha pratyayeṣu san pratyakṣaḥ eva bhavati sa svabhāva viśeṣaḥ satsu ityādi upalambhasya yāni ghaṭāt dṛśyāt pratyaya antarāni teṣu satsu vidyamāneṣu yaḥ svabhāvaḥ san pratyakṣaḥ eva bhavati sa svabhāva viśeṣaḥ tat ayam atra arthaḥ eka pratipattrapekṣam idam pratyakṣa lakṣaṇam tathā ca sati draṣṭum pravṛttasya ekasya draṣṭuḥ dṛśyamānaḥ ubhayavān bhāvaḥ adṛśyamānāḥ tu deśa kāla svabhāva viprakṛṣṭāḥ svabhāva viśeṣa rahitāḥ pratyaya antara sākalyavantaḥ tu yaiḥ hi pratyayaiḥ sa draṣṭā paśyati te sannihitāḥ atas ca sannihitā yat draṣṭum pravṛttaḥ saḥ draṣṭum apraṛttasya taḥ yogya deśa sthāḥ api draṣṭum te na śakyāḥ pratyaya antara vaikalyavantaḥ svabhāva viśeṣa yuktāḥ tu dūra deśa kālāḥ tu ubhaya vikalāḥ tat evam paśyataḥ kasyacid na pratyaya antaravikalaḥ nāma svabhāva viśeṣa vikalaḥ tu bhavet apaśyataḥ tu draṣṭum śakyaḥ yogya deśa sthaḥ pratyaya antara vikalaḥ anye tu ubhaya vikalāḥ iti 
NBṬ_2.15	 anupalabdhim udāhṛtya svabhāvam udāhartum āha svabhāvaḥ sva sattā mātra bhāvini sādhya dharme hetuḥ svabhāvaḥ ityādi svabhāvaḥ hetuḥ iti sambandhaḥ kīdṛśaḥ hetuḥ sādhyasya svabhāvaḥ iti āha svasya ātmanaḥ sattā sā eva kevalā sva sattā mātram tasmin sati bhavitum śīlam yasya iti yaḥ hetoḥ ātmanaḥ sattām apekṣya vidyamānaḥ bhavati na tu heutsattāyāḥ vyatiriktam kañcid dhetum apekṣate sa sva sattā mātra bhāvī sādhyaḥ tasmin sādhye yaḥ hetuḥ sa svabhāvaḥ tasya sādhyasya na anyaḥ 
NBṬ_2.16–2.17	 udāharaṇam yathā vṛkṣaḥ ayam śiṃśapā tvāt iti yathā iti ayam iti dharmī vṛkṣaḥ iti sādhyam śiṃśapā tvāt iti hetuḥ tat ayam arthaḥ vṛkṣa vyavahāra yogyaḥ ayam śiṃśapā vyavahāra yogya tvāt iti yatra pracura śiṃśape deśe avidita śiṃśapā vyavahāraḥ jaḍaḥ yada kenacid uccām śiṃśapām upādarśyaḥ ucyate ayam vṛkṣaḥ iti tadā asau jāḍyāt śiṃśapāyāḥ ucca tvam api vṛkṣa vyavahārasya nimittam avasyati tadā yām eva anuccām paśyatii śiṃśapām tām eva avṛkṣam avasyati sa muḍaḥ śiṃśapā mātra nimitte vṛkṣa vyavahāre pravartyate na ucca tva ādi nimitta antaram iha vṛkṣa vyavahārasya api tu śiṃśapā tu a mātram nimittam śiṃśapā gata śākhā ādimat tvam nimittam iti arthaḥ kāryam udāhartum āha kāryam yathā vahniḥ atra dhūmāt iti vahniḥ iti sādhyam atra iti dharmī dhūmāt iti hetuḥ kārya kāraṇa bhāvaḥ loke pratyakṣa anupalambha nibandhanaḥ pratītaḥ iti na svabhāvasya eva kāryasya lakṣaṇam uktam 
NBṬ_2.18	 nanu tri rūpa tvāt ekam eva liṅgaḥ yuktam atha prakāra bhedāt bhedaḥ evam sati svabhāva hetoḥ ekasya ananta prakāra tvāt trirūpam ayuktam iti āha atra dvau vastu sādhanau ekaḥ pratiṣedha hetuḥ atra dvau iti atra iti eṣu triṣu madhye dvau hetū vastu sādhanau vidheḥ sādhanau gamakau ekaḥ pratiṣedhasya hetu gamakaḥ pratiṣedhaḥ iti ca abhāvaḥ abhava vyavahāraḥ ca uktaḥ draṣṭavyaḥ tat ayam arthaḥ hetuḥ sādhya siddi artha tvāt sādhya aṅgam sādhyam pradhānam atas ca sādhya upakaraṇasya hetuḥ pradhāna sādhya bhedāt bhedaḥ na svarūpa bhedāt sādhyaḥ ca kaścid vidhiḥ kaścid pratiśedhaḥ vidhi pratiṣedhayoḥ ca paraspara parihāreṇa avasthānāt heyoḥ hetū bhinnau vidhiḥ api kaścid dhetoḥ bhinnaḥ daścit abhinnaḥ bheda abhedayoḥ api anyonya tyāgena ātma sthiteḥ bhinnau hetū tatas sādhyasya paraspara virodhāt hetavaḥ bhinnāḥ na tu svatas eva iti 
NBṬ_2.19	 kaḥ punar trayāṇām hetu tvam kasmāt ca anyeṣām hetu tvam iti āśaṅkya yathā trayāṇām eva hetu tvam anyeṣām ca ahetu tvam tat ubhayam darśayitum āha svabhāva pratibandhe hi sati arthaḥ artham gamayet svabhāva pratibandhaḥ iti svabhāvena pratibandhaḥ sādhanam kṛtā iti samāsaḥ svabhāva pratibaddha tvam pratibaddha svabhāvam iti arthaḥ kāraṇe svabhāve vā sādhye svabhāvena pratibandhaḥ kārya svabhāvayoḥ viśiṣṭaḥ iti ekena samāsena dvayoḥ api saṃgrahaḥ hiḥ yasmāt arthe yasmāt svabhāva pratibandhe sati sādhana arthaḥ sādhya artham gamayet tasmāt trayāṇām gamaka tvam anyeṣām agamaka tvam 
NBṬ_2.20	 kasmāt punar sva bhava pratibandhaḥ eva sati gamya gamaka bhāvaḥ na anyathā iti āha tad apratibaddhasya tat avyabhicāra niyama abhāvāt tad apratibaddhasya iti tat iti svabhāvaḥ uktaḥ tena svabhāvena apratibaddhaḥ tad aptṛ ati baddhaḥ yaḥ yatra svabhāvena na pratibaddhaḥ tasya tad apratibaddhasya tat avyabhicāra niyama abhāvāt tasya apratibhanda viṣayasya avyabhicāraḥ tat avyabhicāraḥ tasya niyamaḥ tad avyabhicāra niyamaḥ tasya abhāvāt tat ayam arthaḥ na hi yaḥ yatra svabhāvena na pratibaddhaḥ sa tam apratibandha viṣayam avaśyam eva na vyabhicārati iti na asti tayoḥ avyabhicāra niyamaḥ avinā abhau= a niyamaḥ niyamāt ca gamya gamaka bhāvaḥ na hi yogyatayā pradīpa vat parokṣa artha pratipatti nimittam iṣṭam liṅgam api tu vyabhicāri tvena niścitam tatas svabhāva pratibandhe sati avinā abhāvi tva niścayaḥ tatas gamya gamaka bhāvaḥ tasmāt svabhāva pratibandhe sati arthaḥ artham gamayet na anyathā iti sthitam 
NBṬ_2.21	 nanu ca parāyattasya pratibandhaḥ aparāyatte tat iha sādhya sādhanayoḥ kasya kva pratibandhaḥ iti āha sa ca pratibandhaḥ sādhye arthe liṅgasya sa ca iti sa ca svabhāva pratibandhaḥ liṅgasya sādhye arthe liṅgam parāyattatvāt pratibaddham sādhyaḥ tu arthaḥ apara āyattatvāt pratibandhavidhaḥ na tu pratibaddhaḥ iti arthaḥ tatra ayam arthaḥ tādātmya aviśeṣe api yat pratibaddham tat gamakam yat pratibandha viṣayaḥ tat gamyam yasya ca dharmasya yaḥ niyataḥ svabhāvaḥ sa tad pratibaddaḥ yathā prayatna ānantarīyaka tva ākhyaḥ anitya tve yasya tu sa ca anyaḥ ca svabhāvaḥ ca pratibandha viṣayaḥ na tu pratibaddhaḥ yathā anitya tva ākhyaḥ prayatna ān atarīyaka tva ākhye niścaya apekṣaḥ hi gamya gamaka bhāvaḥ prayatna antarīyaka tvam eva ca anitya tva svabhāvam niścitam atas tat eva anitya tve pratibaddham tasmāt niyata viṣayaḥ eva gamya gamaka bhāvaḥ na anyathā iti 
NBṬ_2.22	 kasmāt punar svabhāva pratibandhaḥ liṅgasya iti āha vastutas tādātmyāt tad utpatteḥ ca vastutas ityādi sa sādhyaḥ arthaḥ ātmā svabhāvaḥ yasya tat tad ātmā tasya bhāvaḥ tādātmyam tasmāt hetoḥ yatas sādhya svabhāvam sādhanam tasmāt tatra svabhāva pratibandhaḥ iti arthaḥ yadi sādhya svabhāvam sādhanam sādhya sādhanayoḥ bhedāt pratijñā artha eka deśaḥ hetuḥ syāt iti āvastutaḥ iti parama artha sattāḥ rūpeṇa abhedaḥ tayoḥ vikalpa viṣayaḥ tu yat samāropitam rūpam tad apekṣaḥ sādhya sādhana bhedaḥ niścaya apekṣaḥ eva hi gamya gamaka bhāvaḥ tatas niścaya aruddha rupa apekṣaḥ eva tayoḥ bhedaaḥ yuktaḥ vāstavaḥ tu abhedaḥ iti na kevalāt tādātmyāt api tu tatas sādhyāt arthāt utpattiḥ liṅgasya tad utpatteḥ ca sādhye arthe ssvabhāva pratibandhaḥ liṅgasya 
NBṬ_2.23	 kasmāt nimitta dvayāt svabhāva pratibandhaḥ liṅgasya na anyasmāt iti āha atad svabhāvasya atad utpatteḥ ca tatra apratibaddha svabhāva tvāt atad svabhāvasya iti sa svabhāvaḥ asya saḥ ayam tad svabhāvaḥ na tad svabhāvaḥ atad svabhāvaḥ tasmāt utpatteḥ asya saḥ ayam tad utpattiḥ yaḥ yat svabhāvaḥ yat utpattiḥ ca na bhavati tasya atad svabhāvasya ātad utpatteḥ ca tatra atad svabhāve anutpādake ca apratibaddaḥ svabhāvaḥ asya iti saḥ ayam apratibaddha svabhāvaḥ tasya bhāvaḥ apratibaddha svabhāva tvam tasmāt apratibaddha svabhāva tvāt yadi atad svabhāve anutpādake ca kaścid pratibaddha svabhāvaḥ bhavet bhavet anyatas api nimittāt svabhāva pratibandhaḥ pratibaddha svabhāva tvam hi svabhāva pratibandhaḥ na ca anyaḥ kaścid āyatta svabhāvaḥ tasmāt tādātmya tad utpattibhyām eva svabhāva pratibandhaḥ 
NBṬ_2.24	 bhavatu nāma tādātmya tad utpattibhyām eva svabhāva pratibandhaḥ kārya svabhāvayoḥ eva tu gamaka tvam katham iti āha te ca tādātmya tad upattī svabhāva kāryayoḥ eva iti tābhyām eva vastu siddhiḥ te ca iti itiḥ tasmāt arthe yasmāt svabhāve kārye eva ca tādātmya tad utpattī sthite tad nibandhanaḥ ca gamya gamaka bhāvaḥ tasmāt tābhyām eva kārya svabhāvyām vastunoḥ vidheḥ siddhiḥ 
NBṬ_2.25–2.26	 atha pratiṣedha siddhiḥ dṛśya anupalambhāt api kasmāt naeṣṭā iti āha pratiṣedha siddhiḥ api yathā uktā yāḥ eva anupalabdheḥ pratiṣedha vyavahārasya siddhiḥ yathā uktā yā dṛśya anupalabdhiḥ tatas eva bhavati sataḥ tasmāt anyatas na uktāḥ tatas tāvat kasmāt bhavati iti āha sati vastuni tasyāḥ asambhavāt sati tasmin pratiṣdhye vastuni yasmāt dṛśya anupalabdhiḥ na sambhavati tasmāt asambhavāt tatas pratiṣedha siddhiḥ 
NBṬ_2.27^1	 atha tatas eva kasmāt iti āha anyathā ca anupalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāpteṣu deśa kāla svabhāva viprakṛṣṭeṣu artheṣu ātma pratyakṣa nivṛtteḥ abhāva niścaya abhāvāt anyathā ca iti sati vastuni tasyāḥ adṛśya anupalabdheḥ sambhavāt iti anyathā śabda arthaḥ etasmāt kāraṇāt na anyasyāḥ anupalabdheḥ pratiṣedha siddhiḥ kutas etat sati api vastuni tasyāḥ sambhavaḥ iti āha anupalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāpteṣu ityādi iha pratyaya antarasākalyāt svabhāva viśeṣāt ca upalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāptaḥ arthaḥ uktaḥ dvayoḥ ekaikasya api abhāve anupalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāptaḥ arthaḥ ucyate 
NBṬ_2.27^2	 tat iha anupalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāpteṣu iti pratyaya antara vaikalyavantaḥ uktaḥ deśa kāla svabhāva viprakṛṣṭeṣu iti svabhāva viśeṣa rahitāḥ uktāḥ deśaḥ ca kālaḥ ca svabhāvaḥ ca taiḥ viprakṛṣṭāḥ iti vigrahaḥ teṣu abhāva niścayasya abhāvāt sati api vastuni tasyāḥ bhāvaḥ iṣṭaḥ kasmāt niścaya abhāvaḥ iti āha teṣu pratpattuḥ ātmanaḥ yat pratyakṣam tasya nivṛtteḥ kāraṇāt niścaya abhāvaḥ yasmāt anupalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāpteṣu ātma pratyakṣa nivṛtteḥ abhāva niścaya abhāvaḥ tasmāt sati api vastuni ātma prti akṣa nivṛtti lakṣaṇāyāḥ adṛśya anupalabdheḥ sambhavaḥ tatas yathā uktāyāḥ eva pratiṣedha siddhiḥ 
NBṬ_2.28^1	 atha iyam dṛśya anupalabdhiḥ kasmin kāla pramāṇam kiṃsvabhāvāḥ kim vyāpārā ca iti āha amūḍha smṛti saṃskārasya atītasya vartamānasya ca pratipattṛ pratyakṣasya nivṛttiḥ abhāva vyavahāra pravarttanī amūḍha ityādi pratipattuḥ pratyakṣaḥ ghaṭa ādiḥ arthaḥ tasya nivṛttiḥ anupalabdhiḥ tad abhāva svabhāva iti yāvat atas eva abhāvaḥ na sādhyaḥ svabhāva anupalabdheḥ sidda tvāt avidyamānaḥ api ca ghata ādiḥ eka jñāna saṃsargiṇi bhū tale bhāsamāne samagra sāmagīkaḥ jāyamānaḥ dṛśtayā sambhāvita tvāt pratyakṣaḥ uktaḥ atas eka jñāna saṃsargī dṛśyamānaḥ arthaḥ tat jñānam ca prayakṣa nivṛttiḥ ucyate tatas hi dṛśyamānāt arthāt tad buddheḥ ca samagra darśana sāmagrīka tvena pratyakṣa tayā sambhāvitasya nivṛttiḥ avasīyate tasmāt artha jñāne eve pratyakṣasya ghaṭasya abhāvaḥ ucyate na tu nivṛtti mātram iha abhāvaḥ nivṛtti mātrāt dṛśya nivṛtti aniścayāt nanu ca dṛśya nivṛttiḥ avasīyate dṛśya anupalambhāt satyam etat kevalam eka jñāna saṃsargiṇi dṛśyamāne ghaṭaḥ yadi bhavet dṛśyaḥ eva bhavet iti dṛśyaḥ sambhāvitaḥ 
NBṬ_2.28^2	 tatas dṛśya anupalabdhi niścitā dṛśya anupalabdhi niścaya sāmarthyāt eva ca dṛśya abhāvaḥ niścitaḥ yadi hi dṛśyaḥ tatra bhavet dṛśya anupalambhaḥ na bhavet atas dṛśya anupalambha niścayāt dṛśya abhāvaḥ sāmarthyāt avasitaḥ na vyavahṛtaḥ iti dṛśyānupalambhena vyavahartavyaḥ tasmāt artha antaram eka jñāna saṃsargi dṛśyamānam tat jñānam ca pratyakṣa nivṛtti niścaya hetu tvāt pratyakṣa nivṛttiḥ uktam draṣṭavyam yathā ca eka jñāna saṃsargiṇi pratyakṣe ghaṭasya pratyakṣa tvam āropitam asataḥ api tathā tasmin eka jñāna saṃsargiṇy atīte ca amūḍha smṛti saṃskāre vartamāne ca ghaṭasya tad tad rūpam āropitam asataḥ iti draṣṭavyam anena ca dṛśya anupalabdhiḥ prayakṣa ghaṭa nivṛtti svabhāva uktā sā ca siddhāḥ tena na ghaṭa abhāvaḥ sādhyaḥ api tu abhāva vyavahāraḥ iti uktam amūḍhaḥ abhraṣṭaḥ darśana ahitaḥ smṛti janana rūpaḥ saṃskāraḥ yasmin ghaṭa ādau sa tathā uktaḥ tasya atītasya pratipattṛ pratyakṣasya iti sambandhaḥ vartamānasya ca pratipattṛ pratyakṣasya iti sambandhaḥ 
NBṬ_2.28^3	 amūḍhosaṃskāra grahaṇam tu na vartamāna viśeṣaṇam yasmāt atīte bhaṭa vivikta pradeśa darśane smṛti saṃskāraḥ mūdhaḥ dṛśya ghaṭa anupalambhe dṛśye ca ghaṭe bhavati vartamāne tu ghaṭa rahita pradeśa darśane na smṛti saṃskāra mohaḥ atas eva na ghaṭa abhāve na api ghaṭa anupalambhe mohaḥ tasmāt na vartamāna niṣedhya viśeṣaṇam amūḍha smṛti saṃskāra grahaṇam smṛti saṃskāra vyabhicāra abhāvāt vartamānasya arthasya atas eva vartamānasya ca iti caśabdaḥ kṛtaḥ viśeṣaṇa rahitasya vartamānasya viśeṣaṇa vat atītena samuccyaḥ yathā vijñāyeta iti 
NBṬ_2.28^4	 tat ayam arthaḥ atītaḥ anupalambhaḥ sphuṭaḥ smaryamāṇaḥ pramāṇam vartamānaḥ ca tatas na āsīt iha ghaṭam anupalambha tvāt na asti anupalambhyamāna tvāt iti śakyam jñātum na tu na bhaviśyati atra ghaṭaḥ anupalapsyamāna tvāt iti śakyam jñātum anāgatāyāḥ anupalabdheḥ sattva sandehāt iti kāla viśeṣaḥ anupalabdheḥ vyākhyātaḥ vyāpāram darśayati abhāvasya vyavahāraḥ na asti iti evam ākāram jñānam śabdaḥ ca evam ākāraḥ niḥśaṅkam gamana āgamana lakṣaṇā ca pravṛttiḥ kāyikaḥ abhāva vyavahāraḥ 
NBṬ_2.28^5	 ghaṭa abhāve hi jñāte niḥśaṅkam gantum āgantum ca pravartate tat etat asya trividhasya api abhāva vyavahārasya dṛśya anupalabdhiḥ pravattanī sādhnī pravarttikā yadi api na asti ghaṭaḥ iti jñānam anupalabdheḥ eva bhavati ayam eva ca abhāva niścayaḥ tathā api yasmāt prayakṣeṇa kevalaḥ pradeśaḥ upalabdhaḥ tasmāt iha ghaṭaḥ na asti iti evam ca prayakṣa vyāpāram anusarati abhāva niścayaḥ tasmāt pratyakṣasya kevala pradeśa grahaṇa vyāpāra anusāryabhāva niścayaḥ pratykṣakṛtaḥ kiñca dṛśya anupalambha niścaya karaṇa sāmarthyāt eva pūva uktayā nītyā pratyakṣeṇa eva abhāvaḥ niścitaḥ kevalam adṛṣṭānām api sattva sambhavāt sattva śaṅkhayā na śaknoti asattvam vyavahartum atas anupalambhaḥ abhāvam vyavahārayati dṛśyaḥ yatas anupabdhaḥ tasmāt na asti iti atas dṛśya anupalambhaḥ abhāva jñānam kṛtam pravartayati na tu adṛtam karoti iti abhāva niścayaḥ anupalabhāt pravṛttaḥ api pratyakṣeṇa kṛtaḥ anupalambhena pravartitaḥ uktaḥ iti abhāva vyavahāra pravartani anupalabdhiḥ 
NBṬ_2.29–2.30	 kasmāt punar atīte vartamāne ca anupalabhdiḥ gamikā iti āha tasyāḥ eva abhāva niścayāt tasyāḥ eva yathā ukta kālāyāḥ anupalabdheḥ abhāva niścayāt anāgatā hi anupalabdhiḥ svayam eva sandhigdha svabhāvāḥ tasā asiddhāyāḥ ābhāva niścayaḥ api tu atīta vartamānāyāḥ iti samprati anupalabdheḥ prakāra bhedam darśayitum āha sā ca prayoga bhedāt ekadaśa prakāśāḥ sa ca eṣā anupalabdhiḥ ekādaśa prakāraḥ ekādaśa prakārāḥ asyāḥ iti ekādaśa prakārāḥ kutas prakāra bhedaḥ prayoga bhedāt prayogaḥ prayuktiḥ śabdasya abhidhā vyāpāraḥ ucyate śabdaḥ hi sākṣāt kvacid artha antara abhidhāyī dvacid prativegha antara abhidhāyi sarvatra eva tu pṛśya anupalabdhiḥ aśabdha upātā api gamyate iti vācaka vyavahāra bhedāt anupalambha prakāra bhedaḥ na tu svarūpa bhedāt iti yāvat 
NBṬ_2.31	 prakāra bhedān āha svabhāva anupalabdhiḥ yathā na atra dhūmaḥ upalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāptasya anupalabdhiḥ iti svabhāva ityādi pratiṣedhasya yaḥ svabhāvaḥ tasya anupalabdhiḥ yathā iti atra iti dharmī na dhūmaḥ iti sādhyam upalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāptasya anupalabdhiḥ iti hetuḥ ayam ca hetuḥ pūrvavat vyākhyeyaḥ 
NBṬ_2.32^1	 pratiṣedhasya yat kāryam tasya anupalabdhiḥ udāhriyate kārya anupalabdhiḥ yathā naḥ iha apratibaddha sāmarthyāni dhūma kāraṇāni santi dhūma abhāvāt iti yathā iti iha iti dharmī apratibaddham anupahatam dhūma jananam prati sāmarthyam yeṣam tāni apratibaddha sāmarthyāni na santi iti sādhyam dhūma abhāvāt iti hetuḥ kāraṇāni ca na avaśyam kāryavanti bhavanti iti kāryāt apratibaddha sāmarthyānām eva abhāvaḥ sādhyaḥ na tu anyeṣām apratibaddha śaktīni ca antyakṣaṇa bhavīni eva anyeṣām pratibandha sambhavāt kārya anupalabdhiḥ ca yatra kāranam adṛśyam tatra prayujyate dṛśye tu kāraṇe dṛśya anupalabdhiḥ eva gamikā tatra dhavala gṛha upari sthite gṛha aṅgaṇam apaśyan api caturṣu pārśveṣu aṅgaṇa bitti paryantam paśyati bhitti paryanta samam ca āloka saṃjñakam ākāśa deśam dhuma viviktam paśyati tatra dhūma abhāva niścayāt 
NBṬ_2.32^2	 yad deśa sthena vahninā janyamānaḥ dhūmaḥ tad deśaḥ syāt tasya ca vahneḥ apratibaddha sāmarthyasya abhāvaḥ pratipattavyaḥ tad gṛha aṅgaṇa deśena ca vahninā janyamānaḥ dhūmaḥ tad deśaḥ syāt tasmāt tad deśasya vahneḥ abhāvaḥ pratipattavyaḥ tad gṛha aṅgaṇa deśam bhitti parikṣiptam bhitti paryanta parikṣiptena ca āloka ātmanā dhūma viviktena ākāśa deśena saha dharmiṇam karoti tasmāt dṛśyamāna adṛśyamāna ākāśa deśa avayavaḥ pratyakṣa apratyakṣa samudāyaḥ vahni abhāva pratīti sāmarthya āyātas dharmī na dṛśyamānaḥ eva iha iti tu pratyakṣa nirdeśaḥ dṛśyamāna bhāga apekṣaḥ na kevalam iha eva dṛśya adṛśya samudāyaḥ dharmī api tu anyatra api śabdasya kṣaṇika tve sādhya kaścid eva śabdaḥ pratyakṣaḥ anyaḥ tu parokṣaḥ tadvat iha api yathā ca atra dharmī sādhya pratipatti adhikaraṇa bhūtaḥ dṛśya adṛśya avayavaḥ darśitaḥ tadvat uttareṣu api prayogeṣu svayam pratipattavyaḥ 
NBṬ_2.33	 pratiṣedhasya vyāpyasya yaḥ vyāpakaḥ dharmaḥ tasya anupalabdhiḥ udāhriyate vyāpaka anupalabdhiḥ yathā na atra śiṃśapā vṛkṣa abhāvāt iti yathā iti atra iti dharmī na śiṃśapā iti śiṃśapā bhāvaḥ sādhyaḥ vṛkṣasya vyāpakasya abhāvāt iti hetuḥ iyam api anupalabdhiḥ vyāpyasya śiṃśapātu asya adṛśyasya abhāve prayujyate upalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāpte tu vyāpya dṛśya anupalabdhiḥ gamikā tatra yadā pūrva aparau upaśliṣṭau deśau bhavataḥ tayoḥ ekaḥ taru gahana upetaḥ aparaḥ ca eka śilā ghaṭitp nirvṛkṣa kakṣakaḥ draṣṭā api tasthān vṛkṣān paśyan api śiṃśapā ādi bhedam yaḥ na vivecayati tasya vṛkṣa tvam pratyakṣam apratyakṣam tu śiṃśapa tvam sa hi nirvṛkṣaḥ eka śilā ghaṭite vṛkṣa abhāvam dṛśya tvāt dṛśya anupalambhāt avasyati śiṃśapātva abhāvam tu vyāpakasya vṛkṣa tvasya abhāvāt iti tādṛśaḥ viṣaye asyāḥ abhāva sādhanāya prayogaḥ 
NBṬ_2.34	 svabhāva viruddha upalabdhiḥ yathā na atra śīta sparśaḥ vahneḥ iti pratiṣedhasya svabhāvena viruddhasya upaladhiḥ udāhriyate yathā iti atra iti dharmī na śīta sparśaḥ iti śīta sparśa pratiṣadhaḥ sādhyaḥ vahneḥ iti hetuḥ iyam ca anupalabdhaḥ tatra prayoktavyāḥ yatra śīta sparśaḥ dṛśyaḥ dṛśye dṛśya anupalabdhi prayogāt tasmāt yatra varṇa viśeṣāt vahni dṛśyaḥ śīta sparśaḥ dūra stha tvāt san api adṛśyaḥ tatra asyāḥ prayogaḥ 
NBṬ_2.35	 viruddha kārya upalabdhiḥ yathā na atra śīta sparśaḥ dhūmāt iti pratiṣedhyena yat viruddham tad kāryasya upalabdhiḥ gamikā yathā iti atra iti dharmī na śīta sparśaḥ iti śīta sparśa abhāvaḥ sādhyaḥ dhūmāt iti hetuḥ yatra śīta sparśaḥ san dṛśyaḥ syāt tatra dṛśya anupalabdhiḥ gamikā yatra viruddhaḥ bahniḥ pratyakṣaḥ tatra viruddha upalabdhiḥ gamikā dvayoḥ api tu parokṣa tve viruddha kārya upalabdhiḥ prayujyate tatra samasta apavārac stham śītam nivartayitum samarthasya agneḥ anumāpakam yadā viśiṣṭam dhūma kalāpam niryāntam apavarakāt paśyati tadā viśiṣṭat vahneḥ anumitāt śīta sparśa nivṛttim anumimīte iha dṛśyamāna dvāra pradeśa sahitaḥ sarvaḥ apavaraka abhyantara deśaḥ dharmī sādhya pratipatti anusmaraṇāt pūrvavat draṣṭavyaḥ iti 
NBṬ_2.36^1	 viruddha vyāpta upalabdhiḥ yathā na dhūma bhāvī bhūtasya api bhāvasya vināśaḥ hetu antara apekṣaṇāt iti pratiṣedhasya yat viruddham tena vyāptasya dharma antarasya upalabdhiḥ udāhartavyā yathā iti dhruvam avaśyam bhavati iti bhūtasya api bhāvasya iti dhaarmi ciśeṣaṇam bhūtasya jātasya api vinaśvaraḥ svabhāvaḥ na avaśyambhāvī kim uta ajātasya iti api śabda arthaḥ jana kaṭ dhetoḥ anyaḥ hetuḥ hetu antaram mudgara ādi tat apekṣate vinaśvaraḥ tasya apekṣaṇāt iti heutḥ hetu antara apekṣaṇam nama adhruva bhāvi tvena vyāptam yathā vāsasi rāgasya rañjam ādi hetu antara apekṣaṇam adhruva bhāvi tvena vyāptam dhruva bhāvi tva viruddham ca adhruva bhāvi tvam vināśaḥ ca vinaśvara svabhāva ātmā hetu antara apekṣaḥ iṣṭaḥ tatas viruddha vyāpta hetu vantara apekṣaṇa darśanāt dhruva bhāvi tva niṣedhaḥ iha dhruva bhāvi tvam nitya tvam adhruva bhāvtivam ca anitya tvam nitya tva anitya tvayoḥ ca paraspara parihāreṇa avasthānāt ekatra virodhaḥ 
NBṬ_2.36^2	 tathā ca sati paraspara parihāravatoḥ dvayoḥ yadā ekam dṛśyate tatra dvitīyasya tādātmya niṣedhaḥ kāryaḥ tādātmya niṣedhaḥ ca dṛśya tayā abhyupatasya sambhavati yatas evam tādātmya niṣedhaḥ kriyate yadi ayam dṛśyamānaḥ nityaḥ bhavet nitya rūpaḥ dṛśyeta na ca nitya rūpaḥ dṛśyate tasmāt na nityaḥ evam ca pratiṣedhasya nitya tvasya dṛśyamāna ātmaka tvam abhyupagamya pratiṣedhaḥ kṛtaḥ bhavati vastunaḥ api adṛśyasya piśāca ādeḥ yadi dṛśya ghaṭa ātmaka tva niśedhaḥ kriyate dṛśya ātmaka tvam abhyupagamya kartavyaḥ yadi ayam ghaṭaḥ dṛśyamānaḥ piśāca ātma bhavet piśācaḥ dṛṣtaḥ bhavet na ca dṛśṭaḥ tasmāt na piśācaḥ iti dṛśya ātma tva abhyupagama pūrvakaḥ dṛśyamāne ghaṭa ādau vastuni vastunaḥ avastunaḥ vā dṛśyasya adṛśyasya ca tādātmya pratiṣedhaḥ tathā ca sati yathā ghaṭasya dṛśya tvam abhyupagamya pratiṣedhaḥ dṛśya anupalambhāt eva tadvat sarvasya paraspara parihāravataḥ anyatra dṛśyamāne niṣedhaḥ dṛśya anupalambhāt eva tathā ca asya evaṃjātīyakasya prayogasya svabhāva anupalabdhau antarbhāvaḥ 
NBṬ_2.37	 kārya viruddha upalabdhiḥ yathā naḥ iha apratibaddha sāmarthyāni śīta kāraṇāni santi vahneḥ iti pratiṣedhasya yat kāryam tasya yat viruddham tasya upalabdheḥ udāharaṇam yathā iti iha iti dharmī apratibaddham sāmarthyam yeṣām śīta kāraṇānām śīta jananam prati tāni na santi iti sādhyam vahneḥ iti heutḥ yatra śīta kāraṇāni adṛśyāni śīta sparśaḥ api adṛśyaḥ tatra ayam hetuḥ prayoktavyaḥ dṛyatve tu śīta sparśasya tad kāraṇānām vā kāryānupalbdhi dṛśya anupalabdhiḥ vā gamikā tasmāt eśā api abhāva sādhanī tatas yasmin uddeśe sat api śīta kāraṇam adṛśyam śīta sparśaḥ ca dūra stha tvāt pratipattuḥ vahniḥ bāsvara varna tvātt dūrāt api dṛśyaḥ tatra ayam prayogaḥ iti 
NBṬ_2.38	 vyāpaka viruddha upalabdhiḥ yathā na atra tuṣāra sparśaḥ vahneḥ iti pratiṣedhasya yat vyāpakam tena yat viruddham tasya upalabdhiḥ udāhartvyāḥ yathā iti atra iti dharmī tuṣāra sparśaḥ na iti sādhyam vahneḥ iti hetuḥ yatra vyāpyaḥ tuṣāra sparśaḥ vyāpakaḥ ca śīta sparśaḥ na dṛśyaḥ tatra ayam hetuḥ tayoḥ dṛśya tve svabhāvasya vyāpakasya ca anupalabdhiḥ yatas prayoktavyā tathā ca sa yabhāva sādhnīyam dūravartinaḥ ca pratipattuḥ tuṣāra sparśaḥ śīta sparśa veśeṣaḥ śīta mātram ca parokṣam vahniḥ tu rūpa viśeṣāt dūra sthaḥ api pratyakṣaḥ tatas bahneḥ śīta mātra abhāvaḥ tatas śīta viśeṣa tū śāra sparśa abhāva niścayaḥ śīta viśeṣasya śīta sāmānyena vyāpta tvāt iti viśiṣṭa viṣaye asyāḥ prayogaḥ

NBṬ_2.40	 kāraṇa viruddha upalabdhiḥ yathā na asya romaharṣa ādi viśeṣāḥ sannihita dahana viśeṣa tvāt iti pratiṣedhasya yat kāraṇam tasya viruddham tasya upalabdheḥ udāharaṇam yathā iti asya iti dharmī romṇām harṣaḥ udbhedaḥ saḥ ādiḥ yeṣām dana vīṇa ādīnām śita kṛtānām te viśiṣyante tad anyebhyaḥ bhaya śraddādiktṛtebhyaḥ iti romaharṣa ādi viśeṣāḥ te na sani iti sādhyam dahanaḥ eva viśiṣyate tad anyasmāt dahanau= vhīta nivartana sāmarthyena iti dahana viśeṣaḥ kaścid dahanaḥ san api na śīta nivartana kṣamaḥ yathā pradīpaḥ tādṛśa nivṛttaye viśeṣa grahaṇam sannihitaḥ dahana viśeṣaaḥ yasya sa tathā uktaḥ tasya bhāvaḥ tasmāt iti hetuḥ yatra śīta sparśaḥ san api adrśyaḥ roma harṣa ādi viśeṣāḥ ca adṛśyāḥ tatra ayam prayogaḥ romaharṣa ādi viśeṣasya dṛśya tve dṛśya anupalabdhiḥ prayoktavyā śīta sparśasya dṛśya tve kāraṇa anupalabdhiḥ tasmāt abhāva sādhanaḥ ayam rūpa viśeṣāt dhi dūrāt dahanam paśyati śīta sparśaḥ tu adṛśyaḥ roma harṣa ādi viśeṣāḥ ca teṣām kāraṇa viruddha upalabdhyā abhāvam pratipadyate iti tatra asya prayogaḥ iti 
NBṬ_2.41^1	 kāraṇa viruddha kārya upalabdhiḥ yathā na roma harṣa ādi viśeṣa yukta puruṣavān ayam pradeśaḥ dhūmāt iti pratiṣedhyasya yat kāraṇam tasya yat viruddham tasya yat kāryam tasya upalabdhiḥ udāharttavyā yathā iti ayam pradeśaḥ iti dharmī yogaḥ yuktam roma harṣa ādi viśeṣaiḥ yuvatam roma harṣa ādi viśeṣa yuktam tasya sambandhī puruṣaḥ roma harṣa ādi viśeṣa yukta puruṣaḥ tadvān na bhavati iti sādhyam dhūmāt iti hetuḥ romaharṣa ādi viśeṣasya pratyakṣa tve dṛśya anupalabdhiḥ kāraṇasya śīta sparśasya pratyakṣa tve kāraṇa anupalabdhiḥ 
NBṬ_2.41^2	 vahneḥ tu pratyakṣa tve kāraṇa viruddha upalabdhiḥ prayoktavyā taryāṇām api adṛśya tve ayam prayogaḥ tasmāt abhāva sādhanaḥ ayam tatra dūra sthasya pratipattuḥ dahana śīta sparśa roma harṣa ādi viśeṣāḥ apratyakṣāḥ santaḥ api dhūmaḥ tu pratyakṣaḥ yatra tatra etat pramāṇam dhūmaḥ tu yādṛśaḥ tad deśe sthitam śītam nivartayitum samarthasya vahneḥ anumāpakaḥ saḥ iha grāhyaḥ dhūma mātreṇa tu vahni mātre anumite api na śīta sparśa nivṛttiḥ na api roma harṣa ādi viśeṣa nivṛttiḥ avasātum śakyā iti na dhūma mātram hetuḥ iti draṣṭavyam iti 
NBṬ_2.42	 yadi ekaḥ pratiṣedha hetuḥ uktath katham ekādaśā abhāva hetavaḥ iti āha ime sarva kārya anupalabdhi ādayaḥ daśa anupalabdhi prayogāḥ svabhāva anupalabdhau saṅgraham upayānti ime sarve ityādi ime anupalabdhi prayogāḥ idam ānantara prakrāntāḥ nirdiṣṭāḥ tatra kiyatām api grahaṇe prasaktaḥ āha kārya anupalabdhi ādayaḥ iti kārya anupalabdhi ādīnām api trayāṇām caturṇām vā grahaṇe prasaktaḥ āha daśa iti tatra daśānām api udāhṛta mātrāṇām grahaṇa prasaṅge sati āha sarve iti etat uktam bhavati aprayuktāḥ api prayukta udāharaṇa sadṛśāḥ ca sarve eva iti daśagrahaṇam antareṇa sarva grahaṇa kriyamāṇe prayukta udāharaṇa kārtsnyam gamyeta daḥa grahaṇāt tu udāharaṇa kārtsnye avagate sarva grahaṇam atiricyamānam udāhṛta sadṛśa kārtsnya avagataye jāyate 
NBṬ_2.43	 te svabhāva anupalabdhau saṅgraham tādātmyena gacchanti svabhāva anupalabdhi svabhāvāḥ iti arthaḥ nanu ca svabhāva anupalabdhi prayogāt bhidyante kārya anupalabdhi ādayaḥ tat katham antarbhavanti iti āha pāramparyeṇa artha antara vidhi pratiṣedhābhyām prayoga bhede api prayoga bhede api prayogasya śabda vyāpārasya bhede api antarbhavanti katham prayoga bhedaḥ iti āha artha antaravidhi iti pratiṣedhyāt arthāt artha antarasya vidhiḥ upalabdhiḥ svabhāva viruddha ādi upalabdhi prayogeṣu pratiṣedhaḥ kārya anupalabdhi ādiṣu prayogeṣu artha antara vidhinā artha antara pratiṣedhena ca prayogaḥ bhidyante yadi prayoga antareṣu artha antara vidhi pratiṣedhau katham tarhi antarbhavanti iti āha pāramparyeṇa iti praṇālikayā iti arthaḥ etat uktam bhavati na sākṣāt ete prayogāḥ dṛśya anupalabdhim abhidadhati dṛśya anupalabdhi avyabhicāriṇam tu artha antarasya vidhim niṣedham vā abhidadhati tatas praṇālikayā amīṣām svabhāva anupalabdhau saṅgrahaḥ na sākṣāt iti 
NBṬ_2.44	 yadi prayoga bhedāt eṣa bhedaḥ para artha anumāne vaktavyaḥ eṣaḥ śabda bhedaḥ hi prayoga bhedaḥ śabdaḥ ca para artha anumānam iti āśaṅkya āha prayoga darśana abhyāsāt svayam api evam vyavaccheda pratītiḥ bhavati iti sva arthe api anumāne asyāḥ prayoga nirdeśaḥ prayoga darśana iti ādi prayogāṇām śāstra paripaṭhitānām darśanam upalambhaḥ tasya abhyāsaḥ punar punar āvarttanam tasmāt nimittāt svayam api iti pratipattuḥ ātmanaḥ api evam iti anantara uktena krameṇa vyavacchedasya pratiṣedhasya pratītiḥ bhavati iti iti śabdaḥ tasmāt arthe tat ayam arthaḥ yasmāt svayam api evam anena upāyena pratipadyate prayoga abhyāsāt tasmāt sva pratipattau api upayujyamānasya asya prayoga bhedasya sva artha anumāne nirdeśaḥ yat punar para pratipattau eva upayujyate tat para artha anumānaḥ eva vaktavyam iti 
NBṬ_2.45^1	 nanu ca kārya anupalabdhi ādiṣu kāraṇa ādinām adṛśyānām eva niṣedhaḥ dṛśya niṣedhe svabhāva anupalabdhi prayoga prasaṅgāt tathā ca sati na teṣām dṛśya anupalabdhi niṣedhaḥ tat katham eṣām prayogāṇām dṛśya anupalabdhau antarbhāvaḥ iti āha sarvatra ca asyām abhāva vyavahāra sādhanyām anupalabdhau yeṣām svabhāva viruddha ādīnām upalabdhyā kāraṇa ādīnām anupalabdhyā ca pratiṣedhaḥ uktaḥ teṣām upalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāptānām eva upalabdhiḥ anupalabdhiḥ ca veditavyā sarvatra ca iti ādi abhāvaḥ ca tad vyavahāraḥ ca abhāva vyavahārau svabhāva anupalabdhau abhāva vyavahāraḥ sādhyaḥ śiṣṭeṣu abhāvaḥ tayoḥ sādhanyām anupalabdhau sarvatra ca iti caśabdaḥ hi śabdasya arthe yasmāt sarvatra anupalabdhau yeṣām pratiṣedhaḥ uktaḥ teṣām upalabdhi lakṣaṇa prāptānām dṛśyānām eva pratiṣedhaḥ tasmāt dṛśya anupalabdhau antarbhāvaḥ kutas etat dṛśyānām eva iti āha svabhāva iti ādi atra api cakāraḥ hetu arthaḥ 
NBṬ_2.45^2	 yasmāt svabhāva viruddha ādiḥ yeṣām teṣām upalabdhyā kāraṇam ādiḥ yeṣām teṣām anupalabdhyā pratiṣedhaḥ uktaḥ tasmāt dṛśyānām eva pratiṣedhaḥ iti arthaḥ yadi nāma svabhāva viruddha ādi upalabdhyā kāraṇa ādi anupalabdhyā ca pratiṣedha uktaḥ tathā api katham ḍṛśyānām eva pratiṣedhaḥ iti āha upalabdhiḥ iti ādi atra api cakāraḥ hetu arthaḥ yasmāt ye virodhinaḥ vyāpya vyāpaka bhūtāḥ kārya kāraṇa bhūtāḥ ca jñātāḥ teṣām avaśyam eva upalabdhiḥ upalabdhi pūrvā ca anupalabdhiḥ veditavyā jñātavyāḥ upalabdhi anupalabdhī ca dve yeṣām staḥ te dṛśyāḥ eva tasmāt svabhāva viruddha ādi upalabdhyā kāraṇa ādi anupalabdhyā ca upalabdhi anupalabdhimatām viruddha ādīnām pratiṣedhaḥ kriyamāṇaḥ dṛśyānām eva kṛtaḥ draṣṭavyaḥ bahuṣu codyeṣu prakrānteṣu parihāra samuccaya arthaḥ cakāraḥ hetu arthaḥ bhavati yasmāt idam ca idam ca samādhānam asti tasmāt tat tat codyam ayuktam iti cakāra arthaḥ 
NBṬ_2.46	 kasmāt punar pratiṣedhyānām viruddha ādīnām upalabdhi anupalabdhī veditavye iti āha anyeṣām virodha kārya kāraṇa bhāva abhāva asiddheḥ anyeṣām iti upalabdhi anupalabdhimat bhayaḥ anye anupalabdhāḥ eva ye teṣām virodhaḥ ca kārya kāraṇa bhāvaḥ ca kenacid sahābhāvaḥ ca vyāpyasya vyāpakasya abhāve na sidhyati yasmāt tatas virodha kārya kāraṇa bhāva abhāva asiddheḥ kāraṇāt upalabdhi anupalabdhimantaḥ evam viruddha ādayaḥ niṣedhyāḥ ubhayavantaḥ ca dṛśyāḥ eva tasmāt dṛśyānām eva pratiṣedhaḥ tat ayam arthaḥ virodhaḥ ca kārya kāraṇa bhāvaḥ ca vyāpaka abhāve vyāpya abhāvaḥ ca dṛśya anupalabdheḥ eva iti eka saṃnidhau aparābhāva pratītau jñataḥ virodhaḥ kāraṇa abhimata abhāve ca kārya abhimata abhāva pratyaye avasitaḥ kārya kāraṇa bhāvaḥ vyāpaka abhimata abhāve ca vyāpya abhimata abhāve
